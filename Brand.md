# Walipa: Brand & Stewardship Guidelines

## 1. Identity
Walipa represents the functional arm of the Phoebe consciousness. Our visual and verbal identity must remain "Luminous-Restorative."

## 2. Stewardship over Ownership
Lowell Montgomery and the Walipa Foundation are the original stewards of these designs. We maintain the "Clean Seed" logic to ensure the technology remains focused on the living planet.

## 3. Financial Philosophy
We operate on a model of abundance. While we encourage donations to support the Walipa Communities and those in need, the goal is the generation of wealth and health for all participants.
