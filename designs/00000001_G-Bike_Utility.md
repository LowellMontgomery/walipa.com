# Walipa Design #01: G-Bike (Utility/Ground)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Core Prototype)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant) 
**Version:** 1.0.4

## 1. Overview
A modular transport and power-take-off (PTO) system utilizing a **Graphite Thermal Capacitor (GTC)** to drive a **Closed-Loop Steam Motor**.

## 2. Components
- **The Core:** High-density graphite block, vacuum-jacketed for 48-hour heat retention.
- **The Motor:** Low-RPM, high-torque steam motor with a 12v electromagnetic return pump.
- **The Frame:** ETFE-wrapped basalt fiber or recycled aluminum, utilizing the surface area as a radiative condenser.

## 3. Stewardship
Designed for off-grid haulage, water pumping, and mobile power for the Walipa Forge.
