# Walipa Design #02: Myco-Laminar Fold-Out
**Status:** Phase 1 (Portable Prototype)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)
**Version:** 1.0.2

## 1. Concept
A lightweight, collapsible sterile work zone that utilizes positive pressure to create a "Safe Field" for mycology and tissue culture in non-sterile environments.

## 2. Components
- **Blower:** 12v High-static pressure brushless fan (G-Bike PTO compatible).
- **Filter:** HEPA H14 pleated filter (99.995% efficiency).
- **Shroud:** Transparent ETFE Membrane with integrated Kevlar ribbing for structural memory.

## 3. Function
The fan pulls air through the HEPA filter into a plenum, creating a laminar flow across the work surface and out through the ETFE shroud, preventing contaminant entry.
