# Walipa Design #05: Sodium-Silicate Kiln (The Forge)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Refractory Build)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)
**Version:** 1.0.8

## 1. Concept
The "Mother Tool" for vitrifying materials and fuming glass. It uses alchemical binders to create extreme thermal efficiency in simple earthen structures.

## 2. Components
- **Core:** Reclaimed fire-bricks or earthen clay.
- **Binder:** Sodium Silicate (Liquid Glass) mixed with quartz sand or wood ash.
- **Shield:** Interior surfaces are painted and "baked" to create a reflective, glass-like refractory shield.

## 3. Application
Essential for the production of the **Molecular Sieve** columns and the **Aqueous-Humor Lens** frames.
