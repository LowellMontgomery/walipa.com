# Walipa Design #06: Aqueous-Humor Lens (Dynamic Optics)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Bio-Mimetic Prototype)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)
**Version:** 1.1.0

## 1. Overview
A variable-focus lens system that replaces heavy solid glass with tensioned membranes and fluid mediums, mimicking the human eye.

## 2. Components
- **Cornea:** High-clarity ETFE Membrane.
- **Humor:** Purified saline or glycerine-water solution.
- **Ciliary Control:** Mechanical tensioning ring to adjust the "Cornea" curvature.

## 3. Use Case
Solar concentration for the **Subterranean Solarium** and atmospheric monitoring from the **Ark Blimps**.
