## 1. Purpose
To archive and share the symbiotic use of local flora, fungi, and insects to restore soil fertility and human health.

## 2. Protocols
- **Fungal Armor:** Mycelium-based remediation techniques.

- **Radicle Rights:** Documentation of management practices that honor the original indigenous stewards.

## 3. Flow
A decentralized knowledge base where every entry is verified by "Stewardship results" rather than "Industrial theory."
