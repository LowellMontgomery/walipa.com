# Walipa Design #10: Subterranean Solarium (Light Tunnels)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Optical Conduit)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)
**Version:** 1.0.2

## 1. Concept
A system for lancing "True Light" (stratospheric solar energy) into protected underground living and growing spaces.

## 2. Components
- **The Collector:** Gimbaled Aqueous-Humor Lens array.
- **The Conduit:** Interior-mirrored tubes fumed with silver or liquid-core fiber optics.
- **The Diffuser:** Borosilicate Sun-Bells to distribute light without shadows.

## 3. Logic
Based on the mirror-logic of the SWOT satellite—mapping and directing energy via fluid and reflective boundaries.
