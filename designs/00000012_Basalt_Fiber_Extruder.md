# Walipa Design #12: Basalt Fiber Extruder
**Status:** Phase 1 (Thermal Design)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)

## 1. Concept
A localized machine to melt volcanic rock (Basalt) and extrude high-tensile fibers for structural frames and cloth.

## 2. Components
- **The Crucible:** Graphite Thermal Core (GTC) powered furnace (1400°C+).
- **Extrusion Head:** Platinum or specialized ceramic bushing with micro-apertures.
- **Winder:** G-Bike PTO-driven spooling system.
