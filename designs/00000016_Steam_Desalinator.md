# Walipa Design #16: Steam Desalinator (Clean Water)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Phase Change)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)

## 1. Concept
Utilizing the exhaust heat from a G-Bike or GTC battery to distill seawater or contaminated water into medical-grade H2O.

## 2. Components
- **Evaporator:** Borosilicate glass chamber.
- **Condenser:** ETFE-shrouded cooling coil.
