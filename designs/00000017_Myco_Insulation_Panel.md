# Walipa Design #17: Myco-Insulation Panel
**Status:** Phase 1 (Curing Protocol)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)

## 1. Overview
Structural insulation panels grown from Reishi or Oyster mycelium on agricultural waste (hemp/straw).

## 2. Finish
Vitrified with a thin spray of sodium silicate for fireproofing and structural rigidity.
