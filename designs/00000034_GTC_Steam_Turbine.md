# Walipa Design #34: GTC Steam Turbine
**Status:** Phase 1 (Thermal-to-Kinetic)
**Logic:** A micro-turbine driven by the closed-loop expansion of bio-ethanol or steam from the GTC core. 
**Form:** A nautilus-shaped housing cast from vitrified basalt-silicate, minimizing turbulence and "grit" in the vapor path.
