# Walipa Design #35: Bio-Polymer Extruder
**Status:** Phase 1 (Form-Giving)
**Logic:** A 3D-printing interface designed to layer hemp-cellulose and mycelium resins into bio-geometric structures.
**Form:** Inspired by the spinnerets of a spider, allowing for non-linear, organic structural growth.
