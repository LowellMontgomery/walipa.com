# Walipa Design #36: Silver Fuming Chamber
**Status:** Phase 1 (Alchemical Finish)
**Logic:** An oxygen-controlled attachment for the Kiln. Uses your graphite rods as electrodes to vaporize ionic silver/gold.
**Form:** A spherical borosilicate vessel fumed with a "Mother of Pearl" iridescent finish to reflect heat inward.
