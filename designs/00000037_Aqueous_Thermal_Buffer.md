# Walipa Design #37: Aqueous Thermal Buffer
**Status:** Phase 1 (Phase Change)
**Logic:** A salinity-gradient tank that stores low-grade heat. It "breathes" heat in during the day and releases it at night.
**Form:** A soft, teardrop-shaped ETFE bladder that expands and contracts with the thermal cycle.
