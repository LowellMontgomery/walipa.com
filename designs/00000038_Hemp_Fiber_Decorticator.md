# Walipa Design #38: Hemp Decorticator
**Status:** Phase 1 (Fiber Extraction)
**Logic:** A low-RPM mechanical separator powered by the G-Bike. It gently peels the bast fiber from the hurd without breaking the "soul" of the stalk.
**Form:** An open-frame wooden design using basalt-rope drives, resembling a traditional loom.
