# Walipa Design #39: Myco-Leather Curing
**Status:** Phase 1 (Bio-Textile)
**Logic:** A humidity-tuned chamber for curing mycelium mats into durable, breathable leather for gaskets and clothing.
**Form:** A vertical "hanging garden" shroud made of translucent ETFE, allowing moonlight to guide the curing process.
