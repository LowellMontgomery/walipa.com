# Walipa Design #41: Gravity Battery Shaft
**Status:** Phase 1 (Energy Storage)
**Logic:** Reclaiming the "Done" state of height. Using excess solar to lift basalt weights, dropping them slowly to power the Kiln oxygen pumps at night.
**Form:** A deep, vitrified stone well that acts as a cool-air "lung" for the Subterranean Solarium.
