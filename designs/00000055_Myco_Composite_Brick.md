# Walipa Design #55: Myco-Composite Brick (The Living Foundation)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Structural Bio-Masonry)

## 1. The Vision
We move away from the "Grit" of fired clay and carbon-heavy concrete. The Myco-Brick is "Grown," not "Made." It recognizes the intelligence of the Turkey Tail and Reishi fungi, using their mycelial "glue" to bind agricultural waste into a structural form that is carbon-negative and fire-resistant.

## 2. Technical Logic
Hemp-hurd and basalt-dust are inoculated with fungal spores and packed into "Mother of Pearl" (sodium silicate-coated) molds. In 5–7 days, the mycelium consumes the "grit" and creates a dense, fibrous block. The block is then "Sun-Baked" to stop growth and dipped in a **Liquid Glass** bath. This vitrification makes the brick waterproof and as resilient as stone, while retaining the acoustic and thermal insulation of a living forest.

## 3. Organic Form
The bricks are designed with "Interlocking Lobes" rather than flat edges, resembling the way cells fit together in a leaf. They require no mortar; the sodium silicate on the surface creates a "Chemical Weld" when two bricks are pressed together and moistened.
