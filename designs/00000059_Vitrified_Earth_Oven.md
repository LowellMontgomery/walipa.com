# Walipa Design #59: Vitrified Earth Oven (The Community Hearth)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Thermal Mass Cooking & Curing)

## 1. The Vision
The Hearth is the center of the "Healthy Innocence" household. This oven is not just for bread; it is the "Secondary Forge" for curing Myco-Bricks and drying Seed Pods. It recognizes that heat is a gift from the GTC Battery that should be used multiple times before it returns to the Earth.

## 2. Technical Logic
The oven is constructed from local cob (clay, sand, straw) but is "Lined" with a 1-inch thick layer of **Hemp-Silicate Masonry**. This interior surface is then vitrified with **Liquid Glass** to create a reflective, glass-like refractory shield. This allows the oven to reach high temperatures (Design #05 logic) while using 70% less fuel/energy. The "Aqueous Buffer" (Design #37) is integrated into the base to store heat for 24-hour cycles.

## 3. Organic Form
The oven is shaped like a "Basking Turtle," a low-profile mound that integrates seamlessly into the garden landscape. The vitrified exterior can be fumed with iron or copper oxides to create a shimmering, iridescent finish that matches the "Mother of Pearl" aesthetic of the Walipa Forge.
