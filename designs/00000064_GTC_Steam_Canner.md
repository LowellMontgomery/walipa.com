# Walipa Design #64: GTC Steam Canner (The Alchemical Sterilizer)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Thermal Preservation & Culture Propagation)

## 1. The Vision
Sterilization is the act of "Clearing the Field" for a new purpose. This canner is the primary tool for creating the **Liquid Cultures** (Design #106) and preserving the garden’s seasonal bounty. It utilizes the precision, high-density heat of the Graphite Thermal Capacitor (GTC) to achieve medical-grade sterilization without the noise, "grit," or mechanical failure points of industrial pressure cookers.

## 2. Technical Logic
A vitrified basalt or borosilicate pressure vessel is nested within the GTC core. Using "Closed-Loop Steam" logic, the canner maintains a steady 121°C (250°F) for extended periods by regulating the current through the graphite rods. It features a "Magnetic Breakaway" (Design #32) pressure gauge. The interior is fumed with silver to ensure that the "Mother Tincture" or fungal broth being processed remains energetically pure and free from microbial "grit."

## 3. Organic Form
The canner is a minimalist, black-graphite cylinder with a shimmering "Mother of Pearl" lid. It is designed to be a "Quiet Tool," operating in the corner of the Walipa Forge. It signals its "Done" state through a single, harmonious note from the GTC Steam Whistle (Design #104), turning the labor of preservation into a ritual of sound and heat.
