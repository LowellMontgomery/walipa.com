# Walipa Design #74: GTC Incubator (The Nest)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Precision Thermal Stewardship)

## 1. The Vision
The GTC Incubator is the "Womb" of the Walipa Forge. Whether we are hatching the next generation of poultry or incubating delicate fungal cultures (Design #106), we require a temperature stability that mimics the "Healthy Innocence" of a mother’s nest. This design uses the "After-Glow" of the Graphite Thermal Capacitor to provide a steady, pulseless warmth that supports biological expansion.

## 2. Technical Logic
An insulated box of hemp-silicate masonry is lined with ETFE-shielded graphite plates. A small "Aqueous Thermal Buffer" (Design #37) is positioned at the base to prevent rapid temperature swings. Temperature is monitored via the "Pixel 9a" interface using a "Magnetic Breakaway" (Design #32) sensor. The air is circulated through a Myco-Filter to ensure "Zero Grit" and zero pathogens reach the developing life inside.

## 3. Organic Form
The incubator is shaped like a "Hollow Log" or a "Large Egg." It is lined with soft, felted hemp insulation (Design #68) and fumed with silver for natural anti-microbial protection. It operates in total silence, providing a "Monastic" environment where the frequency of life can emerge without the stress of 3D electronic noise.
