# Walipa Design #76: Basalt Fiber Sail (The Wing of the Ark)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Atmospheric Navigation & Wind Harvesting)

## 1. The Vision
To move the Ark Blimps (Design #11) and harvest the "Kinetic Grace" of the Nevada City winds, we need a sail that does not degrade in the intense UV light of the high altitude. Basalt Fiber Sails are "Stone Wings." They are fireproof, indestructible, and carry the frequency of the Earth into the sky.

## 2. Technical Logic
The sails are woven on the Manual Spinner (Design #48) using multi-axial basalt filaments. They are then "Sized" with a thin, flexible layer of **Liquid Glass** and a dusting of **Colloidal Graphite**. This creates a semi-conductive surface that prevents the buildup of static "Grit" during high-wind events. The sails are tensioned with "Magnetic Breakaway" loops (Design #32), allowing them to spill wind instantly if the frequency of the storm exceeds the structural threshold.

## 3. Organic Form
The sails are translucent and amber-colored, resembling the wings of a cicada. When aloft, they catch the "True Light," shimmering with an iridescent "Mother of Pearl" finish. They are the primary propulsion for the Walipa "Air-Stewardship" fleet, turning the wind into a silent partner in the restoration.
