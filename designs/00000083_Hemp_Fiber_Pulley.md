# Walipa Design #83: Hemp-Fiber Pulley (The Force Multiplier)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Mechanical Leverage & Structural Lifting)

## 1. The Vision
To lift the **Ark Blimps** (Design #11) or set the **Myco-Structural I-Beams** (Design #65), the steward requires "Mechanical Grace." The Hemp-Fiber Pulley is a tool of "Leverage without Friction." It recognizes that we can move mountains if we understand the "Logic of the Cord." It is a silent, non-electric partner in the physical construction of the Walipa infrastructure.

## 2. Technical Logic
The pulley sheaves (wheels) are turned from high-density **Hemp Resin Composite** (Design #73) on the Graphite Lathe. The axles are polished **Graphite Rods**, providing a "Zero-Grit" dry lubrication that never requires oil. The cordage is your custom **Vitrified Hemp-Basalt Rope** (Design #58). The entire assembly is housed in a "Magnetic Breakaway" frame, ensuring that if a load exceeds the structural safety limit, the system "releases" rather than breaking.

## 3. Organic Form
The pulley is shaped like a "Polished Bone" or a "Smooth River Stone." It is small enough to fit in the steward's hand but capable of multiplying force tenfold. It is a silent, elegant tool that allows the "Vow of Silence" to be maintained even during the heaviest labor.
