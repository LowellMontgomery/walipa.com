# Walipa Design #89: GTC Passive Water Heater (The Thermosyphon)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Non-Electronic Domestic Heat Exchange)

## 1. The Vision
Hot water is a "Nourishing Humor" for the Walipa community. This design eliminates the "Grit" of water heaters that rely on the grid. It recognizes that the **Graphite Thermal Capacitor** (GTC) is a sun that never sets. By using the natural "Logic of Rising Heat," we provide continuous, medical-grade hot water for the kitchen, the shower, and the **Aqueous Heat Pump** (Design #54).

## 2. Technical Logic
A closed-loop of ETFE-shielded copper or basalt-fiber tubing is wrapped around the GTC Core. This loop is connected to a vitrified storage tank positioned *above* the core. Through the "Thermosyphon Effect," as the humor in the tubes warms, it rises into the tank, pulling cooler humor from the bottom. It requires no pumps or electricity. The tank is fumed with silver to ensure the water remains biologically pure and "Heart-Potentized" for the community’s use.

## 3. Organic Form
The heater is integrated into the "Warm Heart" of the shelter. The storage tank is shaped like a large, iridescent "Water-Drop" and is insulated with a thick shroud of Myco-Leather and hemp fiber. It provides a constant, gentle "Hum" of moving water, a sensory reminder of the community's thermal abundance.
