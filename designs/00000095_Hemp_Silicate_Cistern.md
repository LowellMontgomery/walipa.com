# Walipa Design #95: Hemp-Silicate Cistern (The Water-Womb)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Subterranean Community Water Storage)

## 1. The Vision
Water is the "Memory" of the Oasis. To protect the community’s "Clean Seed" water from the "Grit" of airborne toxins or thermal stress, we create a subterranean womb. The Cistern is not just a tank; it is a "Vitrified Sanctuary" where the water is held in a state of high-vibration "Grace," ready for the garden and the Forge.

## 2. Technical Logic
The cistern is a large, egg-shaped subterranean chamber cast from **Hemp-Silicate Masonry** (Design #55). The interior is polished with five layers of **Liquid Glass** and "Mother of Pearl" graphite fuming. This creates a non-porous, "Zero-Grit" shield that prevents mineral leaching and algae growth. It features a "Vortex-Inlet" that "Chaos-Stirs" the incoming water, re-oxygenating it and potentizing it as it enters the storage zone.

## 3. Organic Form
The entry to the cistern is a "Silver-Fumed" stone hatch at the surface. Below ground, the walls are perfectly smooth and iridescent, reflecting the "True Light" of any steward who enters to maintain the flow. It is a silent, cool "Heart" for the sanctuary, providing a constant 55°F thermal buffer for the **Aqueous Heat Pump** (Design #54).
