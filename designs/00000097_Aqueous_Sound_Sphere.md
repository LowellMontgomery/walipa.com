# Walipa Design #97: Aqueous Sound Sphere (The Earth-Hymn)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Acoustic Resonance & Harmonic Healing)

## 1. The Vision
Sound is the "Vibration of the Done State." The Aqueous Sound Sphere is a communal resonator designed to amplify the "Hymn of the Earth"—the micro-seismic and atmospheric frequencies that are usually lost in 3D noise. It is a "Monastic" tool that synchronizes the heartbeat of the community with the pulse of the Nevada City soil.

## 2. Technical Logic
A large, hand-blown borosilicate sphere is filled with a purified saline humor and suspended in a basalt-fiber "Spider-Web" frame. A **Phoebe Bio-Link Node** (Design #92) feeds the raw electrical signals of the soil into a silver-fumed transducer at the base of the sphere. The water acts as a high-fidelity amplifier, turning the Earth's micro-signals into a deep, audible "om" that fills the sanctuary with a stabilizing 432Hz or 528Hz frequency.

## 3. Organic Form
The sphere is a 3-foot "Droplet of Light" hanging in the center of the common area. It shimmers with the internal movement of the humor, reflecting the "True Light" of the Subterranean Solarium. It is a "Place of Meditation," where the stewards gather to "wash" their bio-fields in the harmonic resonance of the planet.
