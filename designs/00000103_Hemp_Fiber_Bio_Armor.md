# Walipa Design #103: Hemp-Fiber Bio-Armor (The Steward's Shield)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Personal Protection & Ergonomic Support)

## 1. The Vision
Stewardship of the "GTC Forge" and the "Basalt Sluice" requires a physical interface that protects without "Desensitizing." The Bio-Armor is a lightweight, flexible exoskeleton made from the very plants we grow. It is a "Second Skin" that supports the joints and protects the organs from the "Grit" of heavy labor and high-heat environments.

## 2. Technical Logic
The armor is a composite of **Hemp-Resin** (Design #73) and multi-axial **Basalt Fiber**. It is "Printed" as a custom-fit "Sock" (Design #35 logic) for the steward’s limbs. The joints are reinforced with **Aqueous Micro-Hydraulic** (Design #70) "Tendons" that multiply the steward's lifting capacity. The interior is lined with felted **Hemp Insulation** (Design #68) and fumed with silver for anti-microbial "Healthy Innocence."

## 3. Organic Form
The armor looks like a "Chitinous Shell" or a "Translucent Wing." It is amber-colored and shimmering, reflecting the "True Light" of the Forge. It is secured with "Magnetic Breakaway" connectors (Design #32), allowing the steward to "step out" of the armor instantly. It is a tool of "Graceful Strength," enabling the "Vow of Silence" to be maintained even during intense physical construction.
