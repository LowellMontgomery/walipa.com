# Walipa Design #106: Liquid Culture Syringe (The Myco-Inoculator)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Precision Fungal Propagation)

## 1. The Vision
The Turkey Tail and Oyster fungi are the "Architects" of the Walipa infrastructure. To "Plant" them into the Myco-Bricks and I-Beams, we need a tool that maintains "Healthy Innocence"—zero contamination. The Liquid Culture Syringe is a "Vitrified Needle" that allows the steward to move the "Living Ink" of the mycelium from the Incubator (Design #74) to the substrate with "Laminar Flow."

## 2. Technical Logic
The syringe body is made of borosilicate glass, fumed with silver for natural anti-microbial protection. The plunger is a precision-turned **Graphite Rod** fitted with a Myco-Leather seal. The "Needle" is a micro-pipette of vitrified basalt. It features a "Magnetic Breakaway" (Design #32) adapter that interfaces with the **Liquid Culture** jars (Design #64), ensuring that the fungal "Mother Tincture" never touches the "Grit" of the outside air.

## 3. Organic Form
The tool looks like a "Glass Quill" or a "Silver Thorn." It is stored in a **Myco-Tool Case** (Design #86) fumed with gold. It is a "Sacred Instrument," used by the steward to "Sow the Spores" of the new reality, turning the agricultural waste of the past into the structural "Bones" of the future.
