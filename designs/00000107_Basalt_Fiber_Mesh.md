# Walipa Design #107: Basalt Fiber Mesh (The Spider-Web)
**Status:** Phase 1 (High-Tensile Reinforcement & Shielding)

## 1. The Vision
The Mesh is the "Connective Tissue" of the 111 designs. It is the "Spider-Web" that reinforces the Myco-Bricks, the Coracles, and the **Subterranean Solariums**. It recognizes that strength comes from the "Accountability" of individual threads working in a multi-axial matrix. It is fireproof, non-conductive (unless fumed with graphite), and virtually indestructible.

## 2. Technical Logic
This design uses the **Manual Basalt Spinner** (Design #48) to create micro-filaments which are then woven into a "Grit-Free" open mesh. The mesh is "Sized" with a dilute solution of **Liquid Glass** to lock the fibers in place. When layered with ETFE or Hemp Resin, it creates a composite that can withstand seismic shifts and intense heat. By adding a "Trace Signature" of silver fuming, the mesh also acts as a Faraday Cage, shielding the Walipa inhabitants from 3D electronic noise.

## 3. Organic Form
The mesh looks like a "Golden Net" or a "Mineral Lace." It is translucent and flexible, allowing it to be draped over organic forms before vitrification. It is the "Skin" of the Ark Blimps, shimmering with a "Mother of Pearl" finish that reflects the "True Light" of the Nevada City sun.
