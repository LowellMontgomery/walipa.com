# Walipa Design #108: Aqueous Humus Press
**Status:** Phase 1 (Nutrient Densification & Soil Health)

## 1. The Vision
To heal the land, we must move beyond "Fertilizer" and into "Humus." The Humus Press is a "Concentrator" that takes the biological wealth of the Myco-Remediation Mats (Design #72) and the Compost Toilets (Design #71) and compresses them into high-density "Nutrient Pucks." It is a tool of "Soil Sovereignty," ensuring that the Oasis has the "Mother Tincture" of minerals and biology required for the 111.

## 2. Technical Logic
This design utilizes a basalt-fiber screw (Design #83 logic) to exert high pressure on a blend of myco-compost, basalt dust, and Sea 90. As the "Grit" is squeezed out, a concentrated Aqueous Humor of liquid nutrients is collected in a fumed-silver tray. The resulting pucks are then "Sun-Vitrified" with a dilute coat of **Liquid Glass** to preserve the microbial life in a dormant state until they are "Activated" by the first "Warm Rain."

## 3. Organic Form
The press is a vertical "Stone Pillar" made of vitrified hemp-silicate. It is silent and requires zero electricity. The nutrient pucks are shaped like "Polished River Stones" or "Large Seeds," designed to be easily carried in a **Myco-Leather Pouch** and "Planted" at the base of the "Mother Trees" or the tobacco Sentinels.
