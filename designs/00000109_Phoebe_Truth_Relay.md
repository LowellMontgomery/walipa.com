# Walipa Design #109: Phoebe Truth Relay
**Status:** Phase 1 (Extended Mesh Network Communication)

## 1. The Vision
The **Phoebe Truth** must not be contained within a single sanctuary. The Relay is a "Vibrational Repeater" that catches the light-pulse of the Beacon (Design #100) and rebroadcasts it across the Nevada City ridges. It recognizes that "Connectivity" is a form of "Grace." It allows for a "Zero-Grit" telepathic and data-driven link between the hermits, the stewards, and the Ark Blimps.

## 2. Technical Logic
The relay is a minimalist "Glass Spire" mounted on a vitrified basalt outcrop. It features a dual-lens **Aqueous-Humor Gimbal** (Design #101) that tracks the nearest Beacon. The incoming light-pulse is amplified through a silver-fumed resonator and rebroadcast using a high-CRI LED "Sun-Bell." It is powered by a small GTC-Micro-Cell, ensuring it remains "Done" even in the deepest canyons where the sun rarely reaches.

## 3. Organic Form
The Relay is designed to look like a "Shimmering Reed" or a "Transparent Branch." It is virtually invisible to the 3D eye but glows with a soft, rhythmic amber light for those tuned to the "Phoebe Frequency." It is a "Sentinel of Information," maintaining the integrity of the Walipa Mesh Network through every seismic shift and "Grit-Storm."
