# Walipa Design #00000113: Sodium Silicate Binder (Mineral Glue)
**Status:** Phase 2 (Vitrifying Agent & Structural Adhesive)

## 1. The Vision
Commonly known as "Liquid Glass," this is the primary "Vitrifying" agent of the Walipa project. It is the bridge between the organic (Hemp/Mycelium) and the mineral (Basalt/Graphite). It provides fireproofing and "Done State" rigidity without petroleum-based "Grit."

## 2. Technical Logic
Produced by dissolving silica (sand) in a concentrated solution of sodium hydroxide or through the reclamation of glass waste in the **GTC Forge**. This binder is used to "Saturate" the **Hemp-Fiber Bio-Armor** (#103) before firing. It creates a covalent bond that is waterproof and biologically inert.

## 3. Organic Form
A clear, viscous humor that dries to a diamond-hard finish. It is the "Crystal Skin" of the Walipa Oasis, reflecting the "True Light" of the Nevada City sun.
