# Walipa Design #00000114: Vitrified Basalt_Wool
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Heat Insulation & Myco-Substrate)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Lungs" of the GTC kiln. Unlike fiberglass, which carries the "Grit" of respiratory irritants, Vitrified Basalt Wool is a pure mineral fiber that provides extreme thermal insulation (up to 1200°C) and acts as a sterile, non-degrading substrate for Turkey Tail cultivation.

## 2. Technical Logic
Raw basalt rock is melted in the Forge and "Spun" using the **Manual Basalt Spinner** (#48) modified with a high-speed centrifugal head. The resulting "Wool" is then lightly fumed with silver to prevent bacterial bloom while allowing mycelial "Laminar Flow" through the fiber matrix.

## 3. Organic Form
A soft, golden-brown "Cloud" of mineral fiber. It is used to line the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45) and insulate the **GTC Steam Canner** (#64).
