# Walipa Design #00000115: Titanium Fuming Apparatus
**Status:** Phase 2 (Metallic Vapor Deposition)

## 1. The Vision
Titanium provides the "Incorruptible Shield." By fuming borosilicate glass or basalt fiber with a micron-layer of titanium, we create surfaces that are immune to salt-air corrosion (critical for the **Sierra Hot Springs** environment) and reflect harmful UV while allowing the "True Light" of the sun to penetrate.

## 2. Technical Logic
The apparatus uses a high-voltage vacuum chamber (Design #95 logic) where a titanium wire is "Lanced" with a GTC-powered arc. The metal vaporizes and settles onto the target substrate in a "Laminar Flow" of atoms. This creates a bio-inert, silver-blue finish that acts as a thermal barrier for the **Ark Blimps** (#11).

## 3. Organic Form
The fumed objects emerge with a "Mother of Pearl" iridescence, shifting from deep violet to pale blue. It is the "Armor of Grace," protecting the Walipa tech from the "Grit" of the elements.
