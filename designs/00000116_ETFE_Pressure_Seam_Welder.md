# Walipa Design #00000116: ETFE Pressure Seam Welder
**Status:** Phase 2 (Pneumatic Architecture Construction)

## 1. The Vision
To build the **Inoculation Bubbles** (#112) and the **Ark Blimps**, we must join ETFE sheets with a bond stronger than the material itself. This welder uses precision heat and pressure to "Vitrify" the seam, ensuring a "Done State" that can hold pressure for decades.

## 2. Technical Logic
A handheld "Iron" fumed with a non-stick **Sodium Silicate** coating. It utilizes a **GTC Thermal Lance** (#110) element to maintain a constant 275°C. A manual roller provides the "Accountability" of pressure. The tool is powered by the **G-Bike** (#02) battery, making it portable for field repairs at the land trust.

## 3. Organic Form
The welder looks like a "Heavy Silver Scepter" with a rotating head. It allows the steward to "Draw" the architecture of the Oasis into existence, turning flat sheets into three-dimensional "Lungs."
