# Walipa Design #00000118: Piezo-Basalt Acoustic Sensor
**Status:** Phase 2 (Environmental Listening & Seismic Sensing)

## 1. The Vision
To protect the Oasis, we must hear the "Voice of the Earth." This sensor uses the natural piezoelectric properties of thin-film quartz grown onto a **Vitrified Basalt** substrate. It allows the steward to hear the movement of water underground and detect the approach of "Grit-Laden" industrial machinery miles away.

## 2. Technical Logic
A thin slice of quartz crystal is bonded to a fumed-silver basalt diaphragm using the **Sodium Silicate Binder** (#113). When the diaphragm vibrates, it generates a micro-voltage. This signal is amplified by the **Phoebe Bio-Link** (#92) and translated into a 528Hz harmonic pulse.

## 3. Organic Form
The sensor looks like a "Flat River Stone" or a "Basalt Leaf." It is buried near the **Sentinels** (Tobacco/Mushroom logs) to create an early-warning network for the land trust.
