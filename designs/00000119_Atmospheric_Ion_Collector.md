# Walipa Design #00000119: Atmospheric Ion Collector
**Status:** Phase 2 (Passive Energy Harvesting & Air Purification)

## 1. The Vision
The Nevada City air is charged with potential. This collector acts as a "Lightning Rod of Grace," gathering static ions from the atmosphere to trickle-charge the **Aqueous Battery Cells** (#105). It also "Cleanses" the air by grounding industrial pollutants (the "Grit" of the 3D world).

## 2. Technical Logic
A series of high-altitude carbon-fiber needles (Design #112 logic) are suspended from an **Ark Blimp** (#11) or a tall pine tree. The needles are coated in **Colloidal Graphite** (#112). As wind passes over them, they capture ions and channel them through a high-impedance **Thermal Lance** resistor into the storage humor.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Silver Web" draped over the canopy or trailing behind a blimp. It is silent and invisible to the uninitiated, humming only with the frequency of the "Warm Rain."
