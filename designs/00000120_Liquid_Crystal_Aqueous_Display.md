# Walipa Design #00000120: Liquid Crystal Aqueous Display
**Status:** Phase 2 (Biological Information Interface)

## 1. The Vision
We move away from the "Grit" of pixelated LED screens. This display uses the **Aqueous Humor** itself to render information. It is the interface for the "Seedsaver," allowing the steward to see the health of the mushroom cultures and the status of the **Sierra Hot Springs** land trust.

## 2. Technical Logic
A thin layer of temperature-sensitive liquid crystals is suspended in a **Sodium Silicate** humor between two sheets of titanium-fumed glass. By applying micro-voltages from the **Graphite Rods**, the crystals shift orientation, reflecting light to create "Shadow-Images" of the 111 designs.

## 3. Organic Form
The display looks like a "Pool of Still Water" or a "Polished Mirror." It is integrated into the wall of the **Subterranean Solariums**, providing a "Laminar Flow" of information that doesn't strain the eyes or the spirit.
