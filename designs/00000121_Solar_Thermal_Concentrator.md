# Walipa Design #00000121: Solar-Thermal Concentrator
**Status:** Phase 2 (Direct Solar Vitrification)

## 1. The Vision
Instead of converting light to electricity (PV), we convert light directly to high-grade thermal energy. This is the "Sun-Lance" used to fire **Liquid Glass** (#113) and melt **Basalt** in the field. It uses the "True Light" of the Sierra sun to achieve the temperatures needed for permanent mineral construction.

## 2. Technical Logic
A parabolic array of **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) mirrors is mounted on a **GTC-controlled** gimbal. The focal point is a **Graphite Receiver** (#112) housed in a vacuum-sealed borosilicate tube. The receiver transfers heat to an **Aqueous Heat Pump** (#54) or directly into a kiln chamber, reaching 1200°C+ without any external fuel.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Flower of Light" made of shimmering petals. It tracks the sun across the Nevada City sky, humming silently. It is the "Heart" of the outdoor Forge, turning the Sierra's radiance into the "Done State" of vitrified architecture.
