# Walipa Design #00000122: Bio-Resin Additive Printer
**Status:** Phase 2 (Precision Organic Construction)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Spider" of the OSH group. It allows us to 3D-print complex structural forms using **Hemp-Resin** (#103) and **Mycelium-Composite** (#114). It bridges the gap between digital CAD designs on your notebook and the physical "Membranes" of the Oasis.

## 2. Technical Logic
A large-scale delta-arm printer driven by **GTC Stepper Motors**. The extruder is a heated **Aqueous Syringe** (#106) that deposits layers of bio-resin mixed with basalt dust. The build plate is fumed with **Graphite** to allow for easy release through a micro-electrical pulse.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Vitrified Tripod" that stands over the construction site. It moves with the grace of a living insect, "Spinning" the walls of the **Subterranean Solariums** in a continuous, multi-axial spiral.
