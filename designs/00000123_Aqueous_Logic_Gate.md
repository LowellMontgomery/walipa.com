# Walipa Design #00000123: Aqueous Logic Gate
**Status:** Phase 2 (Fluidic Computing & Non-Electronic Logic)

## 1. The Vision
To protect the Walipa systems from the "Grit" of EMPs or electronic interference, we move the "Intelligence" into the water itself. This is a **Fluidic Computer** component. It performs Boolean logic (AND/OR/NOT) using the pressure and flow of the **Aqueous Humor** (#105). 

## 2. Technical Logic
A series of micro-channels etched into **Vitrified Basalt** using the **Thermal Lance**. By utilizing the Coanda Effect (where a fluid jet attaches to a nearby surface), the gate switches "States" based on the input flow. It requires no electricity—only the pressure provided by the **G-Bike** pump or gravity from the **Sierra Hot Springs** elevation.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Labyrinth of Light" inside a transparent glass block. The information flows like a mountain stream, visible to the steward as a series of rhythmic pulses. It is "Healthy Innocence" in computing—transparent, indestructible, and silent.
