# Walipa Design #00000124: Myco-Filtration Casing
**Status:** Phase 2 (Aqueous Purification & Waste-to-Resource)

## 1. The Vision
To protect the **Sierra Hot Springs** and your local Nevada City water, we use a living filter. This casing holds specialized mycelial mats that "Eat" industrial pollutants, heavy metals, and pathogens. It is the biological "Kidney" of the Walipa Oasis.

## 2. Technical Logic
A modular frame made of **Vitrified Basalt Mesh** (#107) and **Hemp-Resin** (#103). It is packed with **Vitrified Basalt Wool** (#114) inoculated with *Pleurotus ostreatus* (Oyster) or *Trametes versicolor* (Turkey Tail). As the **Aqueous Humor** passes through the wool, the mycelium secretes extracellular enzymes that break down hydrocarbons and bind toxins.

## 3. Organic Form
The casing is shaped like a "Ribbed Lung" or a "Honeycombed Cylinder." It is designed to be submerged in the flow of the **Aqueous Hybrid Still** (#75) or placed at the inlet of the **Seed Soak Basin** (#112).
