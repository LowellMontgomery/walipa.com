# Walipa Design #00000125: Seed-Ark Cryo-Storage
**Status:** Phase 2 (Genetic Sovereignty & Long-Term Preservation)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Genetic Memory" of the 777. To ensure our heirloom landrace varieties survive the "Grit-Storms" of the 3D transition, we need a storage system that requires zero external energy. This Ark uses the "Deep Cold" of the earth and **Aqueous Logic** (#123) to maintain seed viability for centuries.

## 2. Technical Logic
A triple-walled vacuum flask made of **Titanium-Fumed Borosilicate** (#115). The inner chamber is cooled by a **Phase-Change Humor** (using reclaimed sodium silicate salts). The unit is buried within the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45), utilizing the constant 55°F earth temperature as a baseline. The **Aqueous Logic Gate** monitors humidity and releases a puff of argon gas if a seal is breached.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Vitrified Egg" or "Smooth Pod" with a shimmering silver finish. It is the "Sacred Reliquary" of the Seedsaver, holding the potential for a thousand future harvests in a state of "Healthy Innocence."
