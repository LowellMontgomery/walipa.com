# Walipa Design #00000128: Hemp-Silicate Kiln
**Status:** Phase 2 (Portable Vitrification Furnace)

## 1. The Vision
A kiln that can be built on-site at the **Sierra Hot Springs** using local materials. It provides the controlled environment needed to fire **Basalt Mesh** and cure **Hemp-Silicate Bio-Armor** into its "Done State."

## 2. Technical Logic
The inner chamber is lined with **Vitrified Basalt Wool** (#114). The outer shell is a composite of hemp-hurd and **Sodium Silicate**. It is fired from within using a **Solar Concentrator** (#121) or a **Hydrogen Lancer**. This design allows for "Cold-to-Vitrified" cycles in under 4 hours.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Golden Beehive" with a shimmering mineral skin. It is the "Womb" where the raw elements of Nevada County are birthed into the permanent tools of the 777.
