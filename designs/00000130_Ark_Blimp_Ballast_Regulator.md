# Walipa Design #00000130: Ark_Blimp Ballast Regulator
**Status:** Phase 2 (Pneumatic Altitude Control)

## 1. The Vision
To keep the **Ark Blimps** (#11) at the optimal altitude for the **Ion Collectors** (#119), we need a ballast system that doesn't use sand or lead "Grit." This regulator uses the **Aqueous Humor** itself, shifting weight to navigate the "Laminar Flow" of the Sierra winds.

## 2. Technical Logic
A series of **ETFE Pods** (#116) are distributed along the keel. An **Aqueous Micro-Turbine** (#129) pumps water between the pods and a central **Sodium Silicate** reservoir. By shifting the liquid mass, the blimp can pitch and dive into different thermal layers without venting precious lifting gas.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Spine" of water-filled cells along the belly of the blimp. It moves with a slow, rhythmic undulation, echoing the breath of the steward.
