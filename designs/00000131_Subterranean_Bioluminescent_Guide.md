# Walipa Design #00000131: Subterranean Bioluminescent Guide
**Status:** Phase 2 (Passive Night-Vision & Pathfinding)

## 1. The Vision
For the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45) and the cave systems of the land trust, we avoid the "Grit" of harsh artificial LEDs. This guide uses living bioluminescent fungi to mark paths and emergency exits with a soft, "True Light" green glow.

## 2. Technical Logic
Clear **Borosilicate Tubes** are filled with a nutrient-rich agar mixed with **Vitrified Basalt Wool** (#114). They are inoculated with *Panellus stipticus* (Bitter Oyster). The tubes are sealed with a breathable **ETFE Membrane**. The fungi consume the waste oxygen from the solarium and provide 24/7 illumination without electricity.

## 3. Organic Form
"Glowing Vines" that crawl along the stone walls of the Oasis. They provide a "Healthy Innocence" to the darkness, guiding the Seedsaver through the night.
