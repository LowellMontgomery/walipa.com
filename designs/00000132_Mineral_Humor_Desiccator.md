# Walipa Design #00000132: Mineral Humor Desiccator
**Status:** Phase 2 (Seed Drying & Material Prep)

## 1. The Vision
Before the **Seed-Ark Cryo-Storage** (#125), seeds must be dried to a precise moisture level. This desiccator uses the natural hygroscopic properties of **Sodium Silicate** and **Bio-Char** to pull "Grit" and moisture from the air, protecting the genetic integrity of the 777.

## 2. Technical Logic
A vertical column packed with alternating layers of activated bio-char and vitrified silicate beads. Air is drawn through the column by a **G-Bike Powered Fan**. The moisture is trapped in the mineral matrix, which can then be "Recharged" by placing it in a **Solar Concentrator** (#121) to bake out the water.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Black and Clear Pillar" of minerals. It sits in the processing room of the Oasis, maintaining the "Laminar Flow" of the air and ensuring every seed is "Done" before storage.
