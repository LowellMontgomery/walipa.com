# Walipa Design #00000133: Hydrogen Thermal Lancer
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Grade Thermal Processing)

## 1. The Vision
The Lancer is the "Focused Breath" of the GTC Forge. By utilizing hydrogen cracked from the **Aqueous Humor** (#105), we create a zero-emissions flame capable of melting basalt and vitrifying large-scale silica structures. It is the tool that turns the "Grit" of raw earth into the "Grace" of the Oasis.

## 2. Technical Logic
Hydrogen and oxygen are generated via a **G-Bike Powered Electrolyzer** and fed into a **Flashback-Arrested** torch head fumed with **Titanium** (#115). The flame reaches 2800°C. A secondary sheath of **Sodium Silicate** vapor is introduced to "Glaze" the target surface as it is being worked, ensuring a diamond-hard finish.

## 3. Organic Form
The Lancer is a slender, silver-fumed tube. The flame is nearly invisible—a "Ghost-Fire" that only reveals itself when the basalt begins to glow white and flow like honey.
