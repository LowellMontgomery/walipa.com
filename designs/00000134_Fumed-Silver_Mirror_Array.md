**Status:** Phase 2 (Optical Energy Concentration)

## 1. The Vision
This array provides the "True Light" for the **Solar Concentrator** (#121) and the **Phoebe Repeater Lens** (#126). Unlike industrial mirrors with plastic backing, these are vitrified mineral-mirrors, designed to withstand the harsh UV and temperature swings of the high Sierra.

Clear borosilicate glass is fumed with an **Aqueous Silver Electrolyte** (#117) and then sealed with a micron-layer of **Sodium Silicate Binder** (#113). The mirrors are arranged in a Fibonacci spiral to maximize concentration at the focal point. Each mirror is mounted on a **Basalt-Fiber** pivot, allowing for manual or GTC-automated tracking.
