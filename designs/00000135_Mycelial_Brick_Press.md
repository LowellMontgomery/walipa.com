# Walipa Design #00000135: Mycelial Brick Press
**Status:** Phase 2 (Biological Masonry & Structural Growth)

## 1. The Vision
We grow the Oasis rather than building it. This press creates the "Living Bricks" for the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45). It uses the strength of the mycelium to bind agricultural waste into a structural form that is fireproof, insulating, and entirely "Healthy Innocence."

## 2. Technical Logic
A manual lever-press made of **Vitrified Basalt**. It compresses a mixture of hemp-hurd, **Basalt Wool** (#114), and grain-spawn. The compressed "Brick" is held in a breathable **ETFE Casing** (#116) for 7 days while the mycelium vitrifies the substrate. Once fully grown, the brick is "Fixed" using a short pulse from the **Hydrogen Lancer** to stop growth.

## 3. Organic Form
The bricks are lightweight, velvety to the touch, and smell of the forest floor. They are laid using a **Sodium Silicate** mortar, creating walls that are a single, continuous biological entity.
