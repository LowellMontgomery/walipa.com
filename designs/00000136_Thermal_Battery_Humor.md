# Walipa Design #00000136: Thermal Battery Humor
**Status:** Phase 2 (Phase-Change Energy Storage)

## 1. The Vision
This humor is the "Stored Heat" of the Oasis. By using a phase-change material (PCM) based on reclaimed **Sodium Silicate** and Epsom salts, we can store the high-noon energy from the **Solar Concentrator** (#121) and release it slowly throughout the night to keep the **Inoculation Bubbles** (#112) at a constant 78°F.

## 2. Technical Logic
The humor is sealed within **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) basalt canisters. As it reaches its melting point (approx. 110°F), it absorbs a massive amount of latent heat. When the ambient temperature drops, the humor "Vitrifies" (crystallizes), releasing that heat. It is a "Passive Heart" that beats once every 24 hours.

## 3. Organic Form
A translucent, gel-like substance that turns into a shimmering white crystal as it releases its energy. It is silent, non-toxic, and has a lifecycle of 10,000+ "Done State" cycles.
