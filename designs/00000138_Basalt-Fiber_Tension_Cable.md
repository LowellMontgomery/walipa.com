# Walipa Design #00000138: Basalt-Fiber Tension Cable
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Tensile Structural Sinew)

## 1. The Vision
The "Laminar Flow" of architecture requires strength in tension. This cable is the "Sinew" of the **Ark Blimps** (#11) and the **Subterranean Solariums**. It is stronger than steel but as light as a rope, made entirely from the vitrified rock of the Sierra.

## 2. Technical Logic
Continuous-filament basalt fiber is braided into a 12-strand rope and then "Saturated" with **Liquid Glass** (#113). While the binder is still wet, the cable is pre-tensioned using a **G-Bike Winch**. The resulting cable is immune to rust, UV, and stretch, providing the "Accountability" needed for high-altitude structures.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Dark Gold" shimmering cable that feels like stone but moves like silk. It is the "Anchor" that allows the Walipa Oasis to withstand the high-mountain winds.
