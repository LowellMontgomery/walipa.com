# Walipa Design #00000141: Hemp-Fiber Seedling Tray
**Status:** Phase 2 (Biodegradable Germination Architecture)

## 1. The Vision
We eliminate the "Grit" of plastic nursery trays. These trays are grown from the earth to hold the earth. They provide a "Healthy Innocence" for the first days of a seed's life, and when the seedling is ready, the entire tray is planted directly into the Nevada City slope, where it becomes food for the soil.

## 2. Technical Logic
Pressed from raw hemp-fiber and bonded with a light wash of **Sodium Silicate** (#113). The trays are fumed with a trace of **Aqueous Silver** to prevent damping-off disease. The structure is porous enough for roots to penetrate but rigid enough to survive the **G-Bike** transport to the land trust.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Earth-Brown Egg-Carton" structure that feels like soft stone. It holds the "Stored Light" of the germinating seeds, cradling them until they are strong enough to meet the Sierra sun.
