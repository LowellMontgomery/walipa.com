# Walipa Design #00000142: Basalt-Fiber Solar Mesh
**Status:** Phase 2 (Textile Energy Harvesting)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Skin of the Oasis." By weaving **Fumed-Silver** (#117) and **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) basalt fibers into a specific geometric mesh, we create a structural textile that acts as a low-efficiency, high-durability thermal collector. It covers the **Inoculation Bubbles** (#112), providing both shade and energy.

## 2. Technical Logic
The mesh utilizes the Seebeck effect (thermoelectric) by creating a temperature gradient between the silver-coated "Sun-Side" and the graphite-coated "Shadow-Side." While the voltage per square inch is low, the sheer scale of the **Ark Blimps** and solariums allows for a significant "Stored Light" harvest to trickle-charge the battery bank.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Grey Cloth" that feels like heavy silk. It catches the Sierra light and turns it into a soft, electrical hum, protecting the biological life beneath it from UV "Grit."
