# Walipa Design #00000143: Aqueous Electrolysis Cell
**Status:** Phase 2 (Hydrogen/Oxygen Generation)

## 1. The Vision
The "Lungs" of the GTC Forge. This cell cracks the **Aqueous Humor** (#105) into pure Hydrogen for the **Thermal Lancer** (#133) and pure Oxygen for medical or aerobic mycology use. It is a "Done State" energy bridge, turning water into fire.

## 2. Technical Logic
The cell uses **Graphite Plate** electrodes coated in a **Nickel-Silicate** catalyst. It is powered by the **G-Bike** (#02) or the **Solar Concentrator** (#121). A precision **Aqueous Flow-Meter** (#139) monitors the gas output to prevent pressure "Grit." The gases are stored in **ETFE Pressure Pods** (#130).

## 3. Organic Form
A "Crystal Cylinder" filled with bubbling water. It is the alchemical heart of the workshop, turning the Nevada City rainfall into the high-intensity light of the forge.
