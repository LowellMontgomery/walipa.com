# Walipa Design #00000144: Mycelial Acoustic Dampener
**Status:** Phase 2 (Sonographic Harmony & Sound Insulation)

## 1. The Vision
To maintain "Healthy Innocence" in the Oasis, we must manage the "Grit" of mechanical sound. These dampeners use the natural hollow-tube structure of **Vitrified Basalt Wool** (#114) and the density of Turkey Tail mycelium to absorb noise, creating a "Laminar Flow" of silence.

## 2. Technical Logic
Panels are grown using the **Mycelial Brick Press** (#135) logic, but with a lighter compression. The panels are then "Fumed" with **Sodium Silicate** to provide fireproofing. They are mounted in the **GTC Workshop** to dampen the sound of the centrifuge and high-voltage arcs, protecting the steward's ears and the neighbors' peace.

## 3. Organic Form
"Velvet-Textured Wall Shields" that look like white shelving fungi. They turn the "Grit" of noise into a soft, earthy silence, allowing the "Three Little Birds" to be heard even during heavy work.
