# Walipa Design #00000145: Seed-Sort Optical Aligner
**Status:** Phase 2 (Genetic Quality Control)

## 1. The Vision
To ensure only the most "Vibrant" seeds enter the **Seed-Ark** (#125), we use an optical aligner. It uses the "True Light" of a high-CRI source to detect the density and embryo health of a seed without damaging its "Healthy Innocence."

## 2. Technical Logic
Seeds are fed onto a rotating **Vitrified Glass Disc**. A **Phoebe Repeater Lens** (#126) concentrates light through the seed onto a **Piezo-Basalt Sensor** (#118). The sensor measures light transmission; seeds with low density (indicating poor development) are diverted via a tiny puff of air from the **Aqueous Flow-Meter**.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Glass Wheel" that spins silently. It acts as the "Gatekeeper" of the genetic lineage, ensuring that every seed germinated for the **Sierra Hot Springs** is of the highest vibration.
