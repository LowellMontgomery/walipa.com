# Walipa Design #00000147: Phoebe Truth-Key
**Status:** Phase 2 (Network Authentication & Encryption)

## 1. The Vision
To protect the Walipa Foundation's internal data and the private forums for the land trust, we use a physical "Key." This is the "Individual Accountability" manifest in a mineral form—a "Done State" for digital security.

## 2. Technical Logic
The key is a small **Vitrified Borosilicate** shard containing a microscopic **Fumed-Silver** pattern. When inserted into a **Liquid Crystal Aqueous Display** (#120), the pattern is read by a laser pulse. The specific mineral impurities in the glass create a unique "Genetic Signature" that is impossible to replicate or hack.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Glowing Shard" that can be worn as a pendant. It is the "Anchor" for the steward's digital identity, connecting their physical presence to the global OSH network.
