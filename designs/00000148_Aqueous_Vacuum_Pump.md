# Walipa Design #00000148: Aqueous Vacuum Pump
**Status:** Phase 2 (Pneumatic & Thermal Logistics)

## 1. The Vision
To create the **Titanium-Fumed** mirrors and the **Cryo-Storage** pods, we need a vacuum. This pump uses the Venturi effect within a high-velocity stream of **Aqueous Humor** (#105) to pull a vacuum without mechanical "Grit" or oil contamination.

## 2. Technical Logic
The pump is made of **Vitrified Basalt**. Water is forced through a narrowing nozzle at high pressure using the **G-Bike** (#02). A side-port at the narrowest point of the flow creates a powerful suction. This allows us to evacuate the **Borosilicate Tubes** for solar concentrators, reaching "Done State" vacuum levels using only kinetic energy.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Crystal Hourglass" of water. It sits silently in the workshop, turning the motion of the water into the absence of air.
