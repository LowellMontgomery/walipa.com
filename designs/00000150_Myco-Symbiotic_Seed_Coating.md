# Walipa Design #00000150: Myco-Symbiotic Seed Coating
**Status:** Phase 2 (Biological Enhancement & Soil Seeding)

## 1. The Vision
We reach the mid-century mark with a design for the seeds themselves. This is a "Biological Armor" applied after the **Seed Soak** (#112). It ensures that every seed germinated for the **Sierra Hot Springs** carries its own "Mycelial Bodyguard," protecting it from soil-borne "Grit" and providing immediate nutrient access.

## 2. Technical Logic
Seeds are coated in a slurry of **Sodium Silicate** (diluted 50:1), **Bio-Char** dust, and a concentrated blend of *Glomus intraradices* (mycorrhizal) spores. The coating is flash-dried using the **Mineral Humor Desiccator** (#132). The silicate acts as a protective "Glass Shell" that dissolves only when the soil reaches the perfect moisture level for germination.

## 3. Organic Form
The seeds emerge looking like "Tiny Black Pearls." They are easy to handle, resistant to rot, and carry the "Frequency of the Oasis" directly into the earth.
