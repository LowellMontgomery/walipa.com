# Walipa Design #00000151: Basalt-Fiber Ark Keel
**Status:** Phase 2 (Structural Backbone for Aerial Vessels)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Spine" of the **Ark Blimps** (#11). It provides the rigid accountability needed to mount the **GTC Power System** (#02) and the **Ion Collectors** (#119) while remaining light enough to maintain buoyancy in the thin mountain air of the Sierra. It is designed to withstand the "Grit" of high-altitude turbulence without fatigue.

## 2. Technical Logic
The keel is a hollow-core triangular truss engineered for maximum strength-to-weight ratio. It is constructed from **Vitrified Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138) that are cross-woven and bonded with **Sodium Silicate** (#113). The interior of the truss acts as a primary conduit for the **Aqueous Humor** (#105) distribution, allowing the liquid to double as a thermal mass to stabilize the blimp's internal temperature during day-night shifts.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Golden-Black Skeletal Rib" that runs the entire length of the vessel's belly. Its appearance echoes the fossilized remains of a great mountain bird, providing "Incorruptible" strength. It is the silent, structural "Done State" that allows the Walipa Oasis to take flight.
