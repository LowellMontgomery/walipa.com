# Walipa Design #00000152: Aqueous Heat-Exchanger
**Status:** Phase 2 (Thermal Energy Transfer & Mineral Isolation)

## 1. The Vision
To utilize the volcanic energy of the **Sierra Hot Springs** without introducing mineral "Grit" into our internal systems. This exchanger transfers the "Stored Light" of the earth's heat into the **Thermal Battery Humor** (#136) without any fluid mixing, preserving the "Healthy Innocence" of the natural mineral water.

## 2. Technical Logic
A series of high-surface-area **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) plates are arranged in a counter-flow configuration within a **Vitrified Borosilicate** shell. The titanium fuming creates a bio-inert surface that prevents calcification and scaling from the hot spring minerals. The heat is transferred with 95% efficiency, providing the energy needed for the **Inoculation Bubbles** (#112) without the need for external fuel.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Crystal Radiator" that hums softly as it breathes in the heat of the earth. The transparency of the shell allows the steward to visually monitor the "Laminar Flow" of the humors, ensuring the system is operating in a state of grace.
