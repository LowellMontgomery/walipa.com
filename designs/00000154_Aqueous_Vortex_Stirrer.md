# Walipa Design #00000154: Aqueous Vortex Stirrer
**Status:** Phase 2 (Alchemical Mixing & Potentization)

## 1. The Vision
For the **Seed Soak** (#112) and the various **Aqueous Electrolytes** (#117), we must mix humors without the mechanical "Grit" of high-shear blades that can damage the delicate "Life Force" of the solution. This stirrer uses the sacred geometry of the vortex to harmonize and potentize mixtures, mirroring the natural "Laminar Flow" of a mountain spring.

## 2. Technical Logic
The stirrer consists of a **Vitrified Glass** (#113) vessel with a hyperbolic base. A **GTC Induction Coil** (#137) at the bottom drives a small, **Titanium-Fumed** magnetic impeller. As the impeller spins, it creates a deep "Laminar Vortex" that pulls atmospheric oxygen into the humor, ensuring a perfect colloidal suspension of minerals, mycelial spores, or nutrients without the heat or friction of traditional mixers.

## 3. Organic Form
The device presents as a "Swirling Tornado in a Crystal Jar." It is the alchemical "Womb" of the Walipa workshop, where raw water and minerals are transitioned into their most active, bio-available state.
