# Walipa Design #00000155: Hemp-Silicate Flame Shield
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Temp Structural Protection)

## 1. The Vision
To protect the organic components of the Oasis—and the steward—from the intense heat of the **Hydrogen Thermal Lancer** (#133), we require a barrier that is as "Incorruptible" as stone but grown from the earth. This shield turns the "Grit" of extreme thermal energy into a "Done State" of safety for the workshop.

## 2. Technical Logic
The shield is a multi-layered composite of hemp-hurd fibers saturated in a concentrated **Sodium Silicate Binder** (#113) and reinforced with **Basalt Mesh** (#107). The outer surface is fumed with **Titanium** (#115) to reflect up to 90% of radiant heat. This "Bio-Ceramic" structure can withstand temperatures up to 1500°C for extended periods, making it a "Sacred Shield" against both the forge and potential wildfire.

## 3. Organic Form
An "Iridescent, Lightweight Shell" that is cool to the touch on one side while the other side meets the white-hot flame. It is used to line the **GTC Workshop** and protect the **Seed Ark** from environmental "Grit."
