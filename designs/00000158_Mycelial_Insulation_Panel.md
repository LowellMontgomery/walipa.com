# Walipa Design #00000158: Mycelial Insulation Panel
**Status:** Phase 2 (Biological Thermal Barrier)

## 1. The Vision
We eliminate the toxic "Grit" of fiberglass and petroleum-based foam. These panels provide the "Healthy Innocence" needed for the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45), keeping the Sierra winter cold outside while maintaining the internal "Laminar Flow" of heat. They are grown specifically to fit the contours of the Nevada City slope.

## 2. Technical Logic
Panels are grown from agricultural waste (hemp hurd and wood chips) using *Ganoderma lucidum* (Reishi). Reishi mycelium is exceptionally dense, providing superior R-value and acting as a natural flame retardant. Once the panel reaches the "Done State" of growth, it is dehydrated and "Fixed" with a light spray of **Liquid Glass** (#113) to ensure it remains waterproof and "Incorruptible" by external moisture or pests.

## 3. Organic Form
These present as "White, Velvety Wall Slabs" that are light as air but solid as wood. They create a quiet, earthy atmosphere within the Oasis, absorbing ambient sound and reflecting the "True Light" of the indoor solarium.
