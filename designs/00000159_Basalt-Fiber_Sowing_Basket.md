# Walipa Design #00000159: Basalt-Fiber Sowing_Basket
**Status:** Phase 2 (Manual Symbiotic Distribution)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Seedsaver's Scepter"—the essential tool for the distribution of the **Seed Soak** (#112) blend. It allows the steward to broadcast seeds with a rhythmic flick of the wrist, partnering with the Nevada City winds to restore the north-facing slopes. It is designed to be a bridge between the human hand and the "Radicle Rights" of the earth.

## 2. Technical Logic
The basket is a lightweight, flexible weave of **Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138). The handle is a **Graphite Rod** fumed with **Silver** (#117) for antimicrobial contact. The weave density is varied across the base; as the steward walks and swings the basket, smaller "Wildlife Blend" seeds sift through the mesh naturally, while larger "Guardian" seeds are manually broadcast, ensuring a "Randomized Grace" in the restoration pattern.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Dark, Shimmering Mesh Basket" that resembles a weaver bird's nest. It is the primary tool of the "Symbiotic Distributor," turning a morning walk into a "Restoration Ritual" that feeds both the soil and the local wildlife.
