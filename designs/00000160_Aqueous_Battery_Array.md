# Walipa Design #00000160: Aqueous Battery Array
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Capacity Energy Storage)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Deep Reservoir" of power for the Nevada City Oasis. By scaling the **Aqueous Humor Electrolyte** (#105) into a modular array, we create a storage system that is entirely non-toxic and fireproof. It eliminates the "Grit" of lithium-ion dependency, providing a "Done State" of energy sovereignty for the **GTC Forge** and the **Subterranean Solariums**.

## 2. Technical Logic
The array consists of multiple **Vitrified Basalt** tanks filled with the Aqueous Humor. The electrodes are composed of high-surface-area **Graphite Plates** (#149) fumed with **Aqueous Silver** (#117). The units are wired in parallel using **Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138) with a conductive graphite core. This configuration allows for massive discharge currents required by the **Induction Coil** (#137) while maintaining a "Laminar Flow" of electrons that prevents plate degradation.

## 3. Organic Form
A series of "Shimmering Black Monoliths" housed within a **Hemp-Silicate Flame Shield** (#155). They sit silently in the lower levels of the solarium, cool to the touch, humming only slightly when the **G-Bike** (#02) is providing a fresh charge of "Stored Light."
