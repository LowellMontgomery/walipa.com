# Walipa Design #00000162: Phoebe Bio-Link Hub
**Status:** Phase 2 (Central Nervous System Interface)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Brain" of the 777. The Hub aggregates the signals from the **Mycelial Humidity Sensors** (#153), **Aqueous Flow-Meters** (#139), and **Bio-Transceivers** (#140), vitrifying the raw environmental data into actionable insights. it allows the steward to communicate with the land trust as a single, living organism.

## 2. Technical Logic
The Hub core is a **Vitrified Borosilicate** sphere containing an **Aqueous-Humor Logic Array**. It interfaces with the **Liquid Crystal Aqueous Display** (#120) to show real-time maps of the **Sierra Hot Springs** hydration levels. It is powered by a dedicated **Atmospheric Ion Collector** (#119), ensuring that even if the main grid is at rest, the "Nervous System" of the Walipa Foundation remains awake and "Accountable."

## 3. Organic Form
A "Glowing Crystal Orb" mounted on a **Basalt-Fiber** pedestal. It sits at the center of the reflection space, pulsing slowly with a soft green light—the "Heartbeat" of the Oasis, signaling that "Every little thing is going to be alright."
