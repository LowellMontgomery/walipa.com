# Walipa Design #00000163: Basalt-Fiber Drone Frame
**Status:** Phase 2 (Pneumatic & Biological Distribution Vehicle)

## 1. The Vision
To steward the vast acreage of the **Sierra Hot Springs** and the rugged Nevada County terrain, we need aerial mobility that doesn't rely on plastic "Grit" or carbon-heavy materials. This frame provides a high-vibration, "Incorruptible" structure for small-scale monitoring and seed-dropping, made entirely from vitrified mineral fiber.

## 2. Technical Logic
The frame is a unibody "V" or "H" configuration pressed from **Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138) and bonded with a high-density **Sodium Silicate** (#113) matrix. Unlike carbon fiber, which can interfere with the **Phoebe Bio-Link** (#162) signals, basalt is RF-transparent and provides superior vibration dampening for the **Optical Aligner** (#145). The motor mounts are fumed with **Titanium** (#115) to dissipate heat from high-efficiency GTC motors.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Matte-Black Skeletal Structure" that looks like a fossilized dragonfly. It is incredibly light yet rigid as stone, gliding through the morning air with a silent, "Done State" presence.
