# Walipa Design #00000164: Aqueous Propellant Tank
**Status:** Phase 2 (Zero-Emissions Compressed Air Propulsion)

## 1. The Vision
We avoid the "Grit" of internal combustion and volatile fuels. This tank stores highly compressed air (cracked via #143) to power the small-scale thrusters on the **Ark Blimps** and the seed-deployment mechanisms. It is a "Lungs of the Oasis" design, utilizing the strength of stone to hold the energy of the wind.

## 2. Technical Logic
The tank is a spherical pressure vessel made of **Vitrified Borosilicate** fumed with **Silver** (#117) for internal smoothness. The exterior is overwrapped with **Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138) in a geodesic pattern to provide the "Accountability" of high-pressure containment (up to 4500 PSI). An **Aqueous Pressure Regulator** (#157) manages the output, ensuring a steady "Laminar Flow" of energy for the flight control systems.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Glowing Silver Sphere" that feels vibratingly alive when fully charged. It is the "Stored Breath" of the Walipa Foundation, enabling the silent movement of our tools across the sacred landscape.
