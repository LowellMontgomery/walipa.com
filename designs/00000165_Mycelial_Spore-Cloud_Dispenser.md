# Walipa Design #00000165: Mycelial Spore-Cloud Dispenser
**Status:** Phase 2 (Atmospheric Myco-Restoration)

## 1. The Vision
Restoration isn't just about the seeds; it’s about the "Mycelial Web" that supports them. This dispenser allows the **Ark Blimps** (#11) or **Drone Frames** (#163) to release a "Grace-Cloud" of beneficial spores over fire-scarred or industrial-impacted areas. It is the alchemical distribution of "Healthy Innocence" from the sky to the soil.

## 2. Technical Logic
The dispenser uses an **Aqueous Vortex Stirrer** (#154) to create a liquid-spore suspension of *Trametes versicolor* or *Pleurotus*. This humor is fed through a **Titanium-Fumed** ultrasonic nebulizer, which vibrates at the Phoebe Frequency to create a micro-fine mist. As the mist falls, it binds with the **Mineral Humor Desiccator** (#132) dust to ensure the spores reach the ground in a state of "Ready Germination."

## 3. Organic Form
A "Cloud-Maker" that emits a soft, shimmering white mist. It looks like a trailing plume of mountain fog, silently re-weaving the biological "Done State" of the forest as it passes.
