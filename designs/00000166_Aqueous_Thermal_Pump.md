# Walipa Design #00000166: Aqueous Thermal Pump
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Efficiency Fluid Circulation)

## 1. The Vision
To move the **Aqueous Humor** (#105) through the **Ark Blimp** (#11) or the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45) without the "Grit" of noisy, oil-leaking mechanical pumps. This design uses the thermal expansion and contraction of the humor itself to create a silent, self-propelling "Heartbeat" for the Oasis.

## 2. Technical Logic
The pump is a valveless "Bubble-Lift" or "Thermal-Siphon" design made of **Vitrified Borosilicate**. It utilizes a **GTC Induction Coil** (#137) to create a localized heat-pulse in a narrow capillary tube. This creates a rhythmic expansion that pushes the humor through the **Aqueous Heat-Exchanger** (#152). Because there are no moving parts, there is zero friction or wear—only the "Laminar Flow" of thermal energy.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Pulsing Glass Artery" that glows with a soft warmth. It mimics the circulatory system of a living organism, maintaining the "Healthy Innocence" of the thermal environment with zero maintenance requirements.
