# Walipa Design #00000167: Basalt-Fiber Gearset
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Torque Mechanical Transmission)

## 1. The Vision
Standard metal gears carry the "Grit" of weight and the need for petroleum lubricants. This gearset is the "Mechanical Sinew" of the **G-Bike Utility** (#01) and the **Mycelial Brick Press** (#135). It is lightweight, self-lubricating, and as "Incorruptible" as the mountains from which it was born.

## 2. Technical Logic
The gears are high-precision molded from a composite of short-staple **Basalt Fiber** (#114) and a high-density **Sodium Silicate** (#113) matrix. The tooth profiles are fumed with a micron-layer of **Graphite** (#112) for permanent, dry-film lubrication. This allows the gears to operate at high torque without heat-build-up or wear, ensuring the "Accountability" of the mechanical drive systems.

## 3. Organic Form
"Matte-Black Cogs" that have a shimmering, stone-like appearance. They operate with a silent, low-frequency hum, turning the effort of the steward into the productive work of the Walipa Foundation.
