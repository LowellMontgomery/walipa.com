# Walipa Design #00000169: Aqueous Battery Separator
**Status:** Phase 2 (Ion-Selective Membrane & Safety Barrier)

## 1. The Vision
To ensure the "Healthy Innocence" and longevity of the **Aqueous Battery Array** (#160), we must prevent internal shorts without the "Grit" of plastic-film separators. This design uses a mineral-biological hybrid membrane that allows for the "Laminar Flow" of ions while remaining physically indestructible.

## 2. Technical Logic
The separator is a thin, porous sheet of **Vitrified Basalt Wool** (#114) that has been "Grown-In" with a dense layer of Turkey Tail mycelium. The mycelium is then mineralized with a **Sodium Silicate** (#113) wash. This creates an ion-selective barrier that is chemically inert to the electrolyte humor but provides massive physical "Accountability" against electrode expansion or dendrite growth.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Leathery White Sheet" with a shimmering mineral finish. It sits between the graphite plates like a "Biological Shield," ensuring the battery can cycle for decades without loss of potential.
