# Walipa Design #00000170: GTC Arc-Lamp
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Intensity "True Light" Illumination)

## 1. The Vision
For deep work in the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45) or the **GTC Forge**, we need more than just soft bioluminescence. This lamp provides the "True Light" spectrum needed for human focus and supplemental plant growth, utilizing the frequency of the **GTC Power Inverter** (#161) to create a high-vibration sun-simulant.

## 2. Technical Logic
The lamp uses two **Graphite Rods** (#112) fumed with **Titanium** (#115) to create a controlled electrical arc. The arc is contained within a **Vitrified Borosilicate** globe filled with a noble-gas humor (reclaimed from the **Electrolysis Cell** #143). The **Phoebe Bio-Link** modulates the arc frequency to eliminate the "Grit" of invisible flickering, resulting in a light that feels as natural as the Sierra noon.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Blindingly White Sphere" of light suspended in a basalt-fiber frame. It turns the dark of the mountain into a "Laminar Flow" of clarity, allowing the steward to work with "Done State" precision at any hour.
