# Walipa Design #00000171: Mycelial Resin Filter
**Status:** Phase 2 (Molecular Purification & Fluid Refinement)

## 1. The Vision
To produce the high-purity **Liquid Glass** (#113) and **Bio-Resins** (#122), we must filter out any microscopic "Grit" that could cause structural failure. This filter uses the mycelium's natural ability to capture fine particulates at a molecular level, ensuring the Walipa materials are "Incorruptible."

## 2. Technical Logic
A pressurized canister made of **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138) is packed with a "Filter-Cake" of dehydrated, pulverized Reishi mycelium and **Bio-Char** (#112). The humor is forced through the cake by the **G-Bike** (#02) pump. The complex, branching structure of the mycelium acts as a "Biological Sieve," catching impurities that traditional paper or plastic filters would miss.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Dark Grey Column" that feels as solid as a tree trunk. It is the "Purifier" of the workshop, ensuring that the fluids flowing through the Oasis are as clean as the "Warm Rain" of the high ridges.
