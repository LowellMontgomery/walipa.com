# Walipa Design #00000173: Aqueous Steam Generator
**Status:** Phase 2 (Thermal-Kinetic Energy Bridge)

## 1. The Vision
This generator turns the high-noon energy of the **Solar Concentrator** (#121) or the heat of the **Hydrogen Lancer** (#133) into pressurized steam. It is the "Steam-Breath" of the Oasis, used for the **GTC Steam Canner** (#64) and to power the **Aqueous Micro-Turbine** (#129) when the sun is behind the Sierra clouds.

## 2. Technical Logic
A "Flash-Boiler" coil made of **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) basalt tubing. The **Aqueous Humor** (#105) is injected into the coil in a "Laminar Flow" using a precision **Aqueous Flow-Meter** (#139). The fuming prevents mineral buildup, allowing the generator to run on raw Nevada City rainwater without the "Grit" of scale. The resulting steam is filtered through a **Mycelial Resin Filter** (#171) to ensure absolute purity before it enters the turbines.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Coiled Silver Serpent" housed within a **Hemp-Silicate Flame Shield** (#155). It emits a steady, low-frequency hiss—the sound of the earth’s water transitioning into the "True Light" of kinetic potential.
