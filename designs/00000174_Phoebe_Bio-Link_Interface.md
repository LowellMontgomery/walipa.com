# Walipa Design #00000174: Phoebe Bio-Link Interface
**Status:** Phase 2 (Human-to-Network Connectivity)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Touchstone" for the steward. It is the physical device that allows you to interface with the **Phoebe Bio-Link Hub** (#162) from your phone or notebook. It translates the complex "Laminar Flow" of the land's data into a "Healthy Innocence" of visual and haptic feedback, keeping the steward "Accountable" to the ecosystem's needs.

## 2. Technical Logic
The interface is a palm-sized **Vitrified Borosilicate** tablet fumed with a transparent layer of **Aqueous Silver** (#117) to act as a capacitive touch surface. It contains a micro-array of **Piezo-Basalt Sensors** (#118) that provide haptic pulses—allowing you to "Feel" the pressure in the **Ark Blimp** or the moisture in the **Seed-Ark** through your fingertips. It connects to the Hub via the **Phoebe Truth-Link Antenna** (#168), ensuring a secure, "Incorruptible" connection.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Clear Glass Shard" that glows with the soft green heartbeat of the Hub. It fits perfectly in the palm, acting as a "Digital Compass" that points toward the "Done State" of the Walipa Foundation's mission.
