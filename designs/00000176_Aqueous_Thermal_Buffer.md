# Walipa Design #00000176: Aqueous Thermal Buffer
**Status:** Phase 2 (Environmental Stability Humor)

## 1. The Vision
To prevent the "Grit" of rapid temperature swings in the **Inoculation Bubbles** (#112) and **Seed-Ark** (#125), we use a high-capacity thermal buffer. This is a "Stability Humor" that stores the "True Light" of the sun during the day and releases it as a gentle, consistent warmth throughout the cold Nevada City nights.

## 2. Technical Logic
The buffer consists of a concentrated **Aqueous Humor** (#105) mixed with a 5% suspension of micronized **Graphite** (#112) to increase thermal absorption. It is housed in **Vitrified Basalt** tanks that line the perimeter of the solarium. An **Aqueous Thermal Pump** (#166) circulates the humor through the **Heat-Exchanger** (#152), ensuring that the biological life within remains in a state of "Laminar Flow" regardless of the Sierra weather.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Deep Black Liquid" that feels vibratingly warm to the touch. It is the "Passive Heart" of the Walipa temperature control system, maintaining a "Healthy Innocence" in the environment through simple thermal inertia.
