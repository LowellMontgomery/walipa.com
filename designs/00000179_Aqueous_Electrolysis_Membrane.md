**Status:** Phase 2 (Gas-Selective Ion Exchange)

This membrane prevents "Grit-Mixing" (dangerous gas crossover) while allowing for the high-frequency "Laminar Flow" of protons. The membrane is a composite of **Vitrified Basalt Wool** (#114) that has been impregnated with a **Sodium Silicate** (#113) humor and a trace of **Aqueous Silver Electrolyte** (#117). This creates a Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) architecture that is immune to the degradation common in plastic membranes. It functions as a "Selective Gatekeeper," channelling hydrogen to the **Thermal Lancer** (#133) and oxygen to the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45) with 99% efficiency.
