# Walipa Design #00000183: Mycelial Spore-Storage Capsule
**Status:** Phase 2 (Biological Time-Capsule)

## 1. The Vision
Before the spores enter the **Spore-Cloud Dispenser** (#165), they must be held in a state of "Suspended Grace." These capsules protect the "Healthy Innocence" of the *Trametes versicolor* or *Pleurotus* spores from the "Grit" of UV light and temperature spikes, ensuring they remain "Done" and ready for the Tuesday rain.

## 2. Technical Logic
The capsule is a small, hollow sphere made of **Sodium Silicate** (#113) and **Bio-Char** (#112). The interior is fumed with a trace of **Aqueous Silver** (#117) to provide a sterile, antimicrobial environment. The capsule is designed to be "Aqueous-Soluble"; when it hits the moist soil of the **Sierra Hot Springs**, the silicate shell dissolves, releasing the spores along with a micro-dose of mineral nutrients to jumpstart the "Mycelial Web."

## 3. Organic Form
"Tiny Black Marbles" that feel like smooth stone. They are the "Seeds of the Wood," holding the potential to re-vitrify the forest floor with a single "Laminar Flow" of growth.
