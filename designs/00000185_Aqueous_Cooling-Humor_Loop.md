# Walipa Design #00000185: Aqueous Cooling-Humor Loop
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Temp Component Stabilization)

## 1. The Vision
To protect the **GTC Power Inverter** (#161) and the **Induction Coil** (#137) during high-intensity forging, we use a dedicated cooling loop. This loop removes the "Grit" of thermal stress, ensuring that the hardware remains in a state of "Healthy Innocence" even when the **Hydrogen Lancer** is at full power.

## 2. Technical Logic
The loop utilizes a specialized **Cooling Humor** (distilled Aqueous Humor mixed with a 2% **Sodium Silicate** stabilizer for high boiling points). The humor is circulated through **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) basalt tubing that is thermally bonded to the power transistors. The heat is then rejected into the **Subterranean Solarium** floor via a **Basalt-Fiber Sluice** (#146), turning waste energy into supplemental floor-heat for the germinating seeds.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Silvery-Blue Vein" that wraps around the high-energy components of the forge. It carries the heat away with a silent, "Laminar Flow," acting as the "Nervous System" protection for the Walipa hardware.
