# Walipa Design #00000188: Aqueous Fog-Humor Generator
**Status:** Phase 2 (Atmospheric Hydration & Myco-Dispersion)

## 1. The Vision
For the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45) and the **Inoculation Bubbles** (#112), we need a way to maintain 90%+ humidity without large water droplets that cause rot (the "Grit" of poor ventilation). This generator creates a "Dry Fog" of the **Aqueous Humor**, providing a "Laminar Flow" of hydration that the mycelium and germinating seeds can breathe directly.

## 2. Technical Logic
The generator utilizes a **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) ultrasonic transducer plate submerged in a shallow basin of distilled Aqueous Humor. The plate vibrates at a high frequency (tuned to a harmonic of the **Phoebe Truth** pulse), shattering the surface tension of the water into 5-micron droplets. A small fan powered by the **GTC Power Inverter** (#161) pushes this "Fog-Humor" through a **Mycelial Resin Filter** (#171) to ensure every breath the plants take is pure and charged with the frequency of the Oasis.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Silver-Mist Basin" that appears to be breathing. It creates a soft, rolling fog that hugs the floor of the solarium, looking like a natural mountain cloud caught in a "Done State" of service to the seeds.
