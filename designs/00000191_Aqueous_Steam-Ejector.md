# Walipa Design #00000191: Aqueous Steam-Ejector
**Status:** Phase 2 (Non-Mechanical Vacuum & Fluid Lift)

## 1. The Vision
To move the **Aqueous Humor** (#105) up to the high-elevation **Inoculation Bubbles** (#112) without the "Grit" of electrical pumps, we use the power of phase-change. This ejector uses the steam from the **Aqueous Steam Generator** (#173) to create a high-velocity suction, pulling water from the Nevada City creeks with absolute "Accountability."

## 2. Technical Logic
The ejector is a "Venturi-Style" nozzle assembly machined from a single block of **Vitrified Basalt**. High-pressure steam is injected into a narrow throat, creating a deep vacuum that "Entrains" the cold water. The steam condenses into the water, transferring its thermal energy to pre-heat the **Seed Soak** (#112). It has no moving parts to wear out, ensuring a "Done State" of service that matches the geological lifespan of the Sierra.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Dark, Tapered Flute" of stone. It emits a soft, rhythmic "Chuffing" sound as it lifts the water, acting as the "Mechanical Lung" of the Oasis's irrigation and thermal systems.
