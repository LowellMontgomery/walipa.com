# Walipa Design #00000193: Basalt-Fiber Valve-Spring
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Frequency Mechanical Tension)

## 1. The Vision
To eliminate the "Grit" of metal fatigue and thermal drift in our high-pressure systems, we use a spring made of vitrified stone. This is the "Elastic Heart" of the **Aqueous Pressure-Humor Valve** (#182), providing consistent, "Incorruptible" tension that does not weaken with age or heat.

## 2. Technical Logic
The spring is a helical coil of multi-strand **Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138) that have been "Vitrified" into a specific spring-rate using a high-elasticity **Sodium Silicate** (#113) variant. It is fumed with **Graphite** (#112) for permanent surface lubrication. Unlike steel, it is non-magnetic and chemically inert to the **Aqueous Humor**, ensuring "Individual Accountability" in every cycle of the Oasis's breath.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Black Coil" that looks like a fossilized serpent. It possesses a unique, stone-like stiffness while maintaining the "Laminar Flow" of kinetic energy, snapping back to its "Done State" with zero hysteresis.
