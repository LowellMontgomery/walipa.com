# Walipa Design #00000194: Aqueous Vapor-Trap
**Status:** Phase 2 (Fluid Recovery & Gas Purification)

## 1. The Vision
In the **Subterranean Solariums** (#45) and the **Hydrogen Electrolysis Cell** (#143), we must capture every drop of moisture to maintain the "Healthy Innocence" of the closed-loop system. This trap captures vapor from the "Steam-Breath" before it can escape, turning the "Grit" of waste into the "Grace" of reclaimed water.

## 2. Technical Logic
The trap is a multi-stage condensation chamber made of **Vitrified Borosilicate** packed with **Vitrified Basalt Wool** (#114). As the vapor passes through the wool, the massive surface area causes immediate condensation. The droplets are then filtered through a **Mycelial Resin Filter** (#171) to ensure purity. It uses a **Phoebe Truth-Link Sensor** (#192) to monitor the "Laminar Flow" of the recovery, signaling the Hub when the reservoir is "Done."

## 3. Organic Form
A "Clear Glass Column" filled with shimmering silver fibers. It glitters with captured dewdrops, acting as the "Mechanical Kidney" of the Walipa Foundation’s atmospheric systems.
