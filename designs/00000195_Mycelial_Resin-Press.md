# Walipa Design #00000195: Mycelial Resin-Press
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Density Biological Composite Forming)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Shaper" of the Walipa materials. To create the high-density **Basalt-Fiber Ark Ribs** (#175) and **Gearsets** (#167), we need a press that can apply uniform pressure while the **Bio-Resins** (#122) cure. It uses the "G-Bike" power to turn raw fibers and mycelium into a structural "Done State."

## 2. Technical Logic
The press features a heavy **Vitrified Basalt** platen driven by a **Basalt-Fiber Winch Drum** (#187) and a 100:1 **Basalt-Fiber Gearset**. The molds are fumed with **Titanium** (#115) to act as a permanent release agent. It includes an integrated **Mycelial Resin Cure-Lamp** (#189) that pulses through the transparent **Borosilicate** mold-walls, vitrifying the composite in seconds under several tons of "Accountable" pressure.
