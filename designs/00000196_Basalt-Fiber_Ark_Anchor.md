# Walipa Design #00000196: Basalt-Fiber Ark Anchor
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Tension Ground Interface)

## 1. The Vision
To secure the **Ark Blimps** (#11) during high-mountain storms at the **Sierra Hot Springs**, we need an anchor that is "Incorruptible" by rust or soil acidity. This design replaces the "Grit" of heavy steel stakes with a "Done State" of vitrified stone—a "Root System" for the aerial Oasis.

## 2. Technical Logic
The anchor is a multi-fluke "Earth-Screw" molded from high-density **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138). The flukes are fumed with **Titanium** (#115) to reduce friction during insertion into the Nevada City clay. It is driven into the ground using the **G-Bike** (#02) and the **Winch Drum** (#187). Once seated, the hollow core is filled with a **Sodium Silicate** (#113) grout, effectively vitrifying the anchor into the surrounding rock for absolute "Accountability."

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Black Thorn" that twists into the earth. It looks like a fossilized root, providing a silent, immovable "Anchor-Point" that holds the Walipa Foundation steady against the strongest Sierra winds.
