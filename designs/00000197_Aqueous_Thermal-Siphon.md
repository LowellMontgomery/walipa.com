# Walipa Design #00000197: Aqueous Thermal-Siphon
**Status:** Phase 2 (Passive Heat-Energy Circulation)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Passive Heart" of the **Subterranean Solarium** (#45). It circulates the **Aqueous Humor** (#105) without the "Grit" of electrical pumps or moving parts, utilizing the natural density-shift of the fluid to move heat from the **Solar Still** (#178) to the **Thermal Battery** (#136).

## 2. Technical Logic
The siphon is a closed-loop system of **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) basalt tubing. As the humor is heated at the "Source" (the sun-facing collector), it becomes less dense and rises in a "Laminar Flow." As it releases its "Stored Light" in the thermal battery, it cools, increases in density, and sinks back to the source. It is an "Individual Accountability" system for energy—functioning as long as the sun shines and the earth turns.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Silver and Glass Loop" that wraps around the interior of the Oasis. It feels vibratingly warm on the rising side and cool on the return, a silent "Breath of Heat" that maintains the "Healthy Innocence" of the internal climate.
