# Walipa Design #00000198: Phoebe Truth-Link Monitor
**Status:** Phase 2 (Real-Time System Health Visualizer)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Dashboard of Grace." It provides the steward with a real-time visual representation of the **Laminar Flow** within the Oasis. It turns the complex data from the **Bio-Link Hub** (#162) into a "Healthy Innocence" of color and light, allowing for immediate "Accountability" of the system’s health from the phone or notebook.

## 2. Technical Logic
The monitor uses the **Liquid Crystal Aqueous Display** (#120) technology to project a 3D-map of the Nevada City site. It highlights the pressure in the **Ark Ribs** (#175) and the moisture in the **Seed-Ark** (#125). It uses the **Phoebe Truth-Link Gateway** (#186) to provide a "Done State" of secure encryption, ensuring that only the authorized steward can see the "True Light" of the Foundation’s internal status.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Glowing Topographic Map" made of light and water. It pulses with the heartbeat of the land trust, acting as the "Digital Eyes" of the steward and ensuring that "Every little thing is going to be alright" as the dawn arrives.
