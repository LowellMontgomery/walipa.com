# Walipa Design #00000199: Basalt-Fiber Ark Deck
**Status:** Phase 2 (Aeronautical Flooring & Equipment Mounting)

## 1. The Vision
The "Soil of the Sky." This deck provides the stable, lightweight platform for the steward and the **GTC Forge** within the **Ark Blimp** (#11). It must handle the "Grit" of heavy equipment while remaining light enough to maintain a "Laminar Flow" of buoyancy. It is the "Done State" for aerial habitation.

## 2. Technical Logic
The deck is a honeycomb-core sandwich panel. The skins are made of bi-directional **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138) bonded with a high-impact **Sodium Silicate** (#113) matrix. The core is composed of recycled **ETFE** (#116) cells. The surface is fumed with a coarse-grain **Titanium** (#115) to provide a permanent, non-slip texture without the need for toxic paints or rubber mats. It is fireproof, rot-proof, and "Incorruptible" by the high-mountain elements.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Grey-Silver Floor" that feels as solid as a mountain ledge but is light enough to float. It provides the "Accountability" for all onboard systems, acting as the foundation for the Walipa Foundation's aerial operations.
