# Walipa Design #00000200: Aqueous_Vapor-Condenser
**Status:** Phase 2 (Closed-Loop Atmospheric Recovery)

## 1. The Vision
We mark the **200** milestone with the "Breath-Catcher." In the high-altitude environment of the **Ark Blimp**, water is a precious humor. This condenser captures the humidity from the steward's breath and the **Aqueous Steam Generator** (#173), turning the "Grit" of waste heat into the "Grace" of reclaimed water.

## 2. Technical Logic
The condenser is a multi-stage heat exchanger made of **Vitrified Borosilicate**. It utilizes the "Cold Sink" of the high-altitude Sierra air to chill **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) basalt coils. As moist air passes over the coils, the water vitrifies into droplets and is collected in a **Basalt-Fiber Sluice** (#146). It is monitored by a **Phoebe Truth-Link Sensor** (#192) to ensure the "Laminar Flow" of recovery is 100% efficient, maintaining a "Healthy Innocence" in the vessel's atmosphere.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Crystal Lattice of Silver Coils" that glitters with constant condensation. It looks like a frost-covered branch caught in a "Done State" of service, providing the "Life-Force" needed for long-duration stewardship missions.
