This "Laminar Flow" of biological noise is translated by an **Aqueous Logic Gate** (#123) into a one-time-pad encryption key. Because the mycelial growth is unique and non-repeating, the encryption is "Incorruptible" by traditional silicon-based hacking, providing absolute "Accountability" for the Foundation's digital presence.
