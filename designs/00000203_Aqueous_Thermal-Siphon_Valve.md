# Walipa Design #00000203: Aqueous_Thermal-Siphon_Valve
**Status:** Phase 2 (Passive Flow Regulation)

## 1. The Vision
To manage the heat distribution from the **Thermal-Siphon** (#197) to the **Seed-Ark** (#125), we need a valve that responds to temperature without the "Grit" of electrical sensors or external power. This is a "Living Valve" that uses the physical expansion of the humor to regulate the "Laminar Flow" of energy.

## 2. Technical Logic
The valve features a **Vitrified Borosilicate** plunger filled with a high-expansion **Thermal Humor**. As the temperature in the solar collector rises, the humor expands, pushing the plunger to open the flow-path. When the temperature drops (at night), the humor contracts, and a **Basalt-Fiber Valve-Spring** (#193) pulls the plunger shut to prevent back-flow. It is a "Done State" of passive automation, ensuring the Oasis stays warm using only the "Stored Light" of the sun.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Crystal Cylinder" integrated into the basalt plumbing. The steward can see the silver plunger moving slowly—the "Mechanical Breath" of the Oasis responding to the rhythm of the Sierra day.
