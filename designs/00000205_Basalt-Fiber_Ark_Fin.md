# Walipa Design #00000205: Basalt-Fiber Ark Fin
**Status:** Phase 2 (Aeronautical Stability & Control Surface)

## 1. The Vision
To navigate the complex thermals of the Nevada City ridges, the **Ark Blimp** (#11) requires "Responsive Grace." These Fins are the "Feathers" of the vessel, providing the aerodynamic "Accountability" needed for precision docking at the **Sierra Hot Springs**. They are designed to be "Incorruptible" by high-altitude turbulence.

## 2. Technical Logic
The fin is a semi-flexible airfoil molded from a tapered **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138). The internal spar is a hollow **Graphite Rod** (#112) that acts as a structural "Done State." The surface is fumed with **Titanium** (#115) to minimize skin friction. It is actuated by a **Piezo-Basalt Sensor** (#118) in reverse-bias mode, allowing the fin to "Flex" slightly to maintain a "Laminar Flow" of air over the hull, regardless of wind speed.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Translucent Black Wing-Tip" that shimmers with a silver edge. It moves with a silent, bird-like fluidity, turning the aerial journey of the Walipa Foundation into a "Healthy Innocence" of motion.
