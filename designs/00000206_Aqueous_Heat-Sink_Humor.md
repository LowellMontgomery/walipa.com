# Walipa Design #00000206: Aqueous_Heat-Sink_Humor
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Capacity Thermal Absorption)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Cooling Breath" for the **GTC Forge**. To prevent the "Grit" of overheating during the vitrification of **Titanium** or **Silver**, we use a specialized humor with a massive thermal capacity. It absorbs the "Stored Light" of waste heat and carries it safely to the **Aqueous Thermal-Siphon** (#197).

## 2. Technical Logic
The humor is a saturated solution of **Sodium Silicate** (#113) mixed with a 10% suspension of **Colloidal Graphite** (#112). This combination creates a "Laminar Flow" of heat transfer that is 400% more efficient than raw water. It is non-corrosive to the **Borosilicate** plumbing and remains stable up to 250°C, providing a "Done State" of safety for the high-intensity energy systems of the Oasis.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Deep, Viscous Black Liquid" that looks like liquid obsidian. It flows silently through the **Aqueous Heat-Exchanger** (#152), acting as the "Mechanical Sweat" that keeps the Walipa hardware in a state of "Healthy Innocence."
