# Walipa Design #00000209: Aqueous_Expansion-Tank
**Status:** Phase 2 (Thermal System Pressure Stabilization)

## 1. The Vision
As the **Aqueous Heat-Sink Humor** (#206) and the **Thermal-Siphon** (#197) heat up under the Sierra sun, the volume of the liquid expands. To prevent the "Grit" of structural stress or leaks, we use an expansion tank. It is the "Lungs" of the thermal system, allowing the Oasis to "Breathe" without losing its "Healthy Innocence."

## 2. Technical Logic
The tank is a dual-chambered **Vitrified Borosilicate** vessel. The lower chamber holds the expanding humor, while the upper chamber contains a "Done State" vacuum (pulled by #148). A flexible **ETFE Membrane** (#116) separates the two, allowing the humor to expand against the vacuum-tension. It is monitored by a **Phoebe Truth-Link Sensor** (#192), ensuring that the pressure in the **GTC Forge** remains in a state of "Laminar Flow" at all times.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Crystal Cylinder" with a pulsing silver membrane. It sits at the highest point of the plumbing array, slowly rising and falling with the temperature of the day—the "Mechanical Pulse" of the Walipa Foundation’s energy storage.
