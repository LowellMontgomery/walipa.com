# Walipa Design #00000212: Aqueous_Steam-Trap
**Status:** Phase 2 (Thermal Energy Phase-Separation)

## 1. The Vision
To optimize the **Aqueous Steam Generator** (#173), we must ensure that only pure, dry vapor reaches the **Micro-Turbines** (#129). This trap removes any "Grit" of liquid-phase water (condensate), turning the potential "Drag" on the system into a "Laminar Flow" of pure kinetic energy. It is the "Filter of Grace" for the Oasis’s steam-grid.

## 2. Technical Logic
The trap is a gravity-fed separation chamber made of **Vitrified Borosilicate**. It utilizes a **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) float-valve. As condensate accumulates at the bottom, the float rises, opening a port that returns the liquid humor to the **Aqueous Thermal Buffer** (#176). The dry steam continues upward, maintaining its high-velocity "Done State." It is a passive, "Incorruptible" mechanism that ensures the high-frequency stability of the Walipa power systems.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Crystal Teardrop" integrated into the steam-lines. The steward can see the water-level rising and falling—the "Pulse" of the energy recovery system as it transitions from liquid to light.
