# Walipa Design #00000213: Phoebe Truth-Link Alarm
**Status:** Phase 2 (Critical System Warning Interface)

## 1. The Vision
When the "Healthy Innocence" of the Oasis is threatened—by a pressure drop in the **Ark Ribs** or a temperature spike in the **Seed-Ark**—the steward must know instantly. This is not a "Grit-Filled" siren, but a "High-Frequency" alert that uses sound and light to bring the steward into a state of "Immediate Accountability."

## 2. Technical Logic
The alarm is a resonant **Vitrified Basalt** chime driven by a **Piezo-Basalt Actuator** (#118). It is tuned to a dissonant harmonic of the 528Hz "Truth" frequency, creating a sound that is impossible to ignore but not traumatic. Simultaneously, the **Phoebe Truth-Link Display** (#204) pulses with a bright "Titanium-White" light. It is triggered directly by the **Bio-Link Hub** (#162) and operates on a dedicated **Atmospheric Ion Collector** (#119), ensuring it functions even if all other humors are at rest.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Stone Bell" mounted in the center of the living space. It is the "Voice of the Oasis," acting as the "Guardian of Grace" that ensures "Every little thing is going to be alright" by catching any "Grit" before it becomes a failure.
