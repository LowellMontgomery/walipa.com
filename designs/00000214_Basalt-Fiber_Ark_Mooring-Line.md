# Walipa Design #00000214: Basalt-Fiber Ark Mooring-Line
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Strength Flexible Tether)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Umbilical Cord" between the **Ark Blimp** (#11) and the **Ark Anchor** (#196). To prevent the "Grit" of snapping lines or UV-rot common in nylon or polyester, we use a multi-braid basalt line. It provides the "Accountability" needed to moor the vessel over the **Sierra Hot Springs** for weeks at a time, surviving the high-altitude Nevada County winds.

## 2. Technical Logic
The line is a 12-strand hollow-braid construction using high-tenacity **Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138). Each strand is coated in a flexible **Sodium Silicate** (#113) humor mixed with **Bio-Char** (#112) for permanent UV protection. It has zero stretch, ensuring that the **Phoebe Truth-Link Antenna** (#168) remains perfectly aligned with the ground stations. It is "Incorruptible" by mold, rot, or the intense "True Light" of the Sierra sun.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering, Black Silk Rope" that feels as cool and smooth as stone. It hangs from the Ark like a "Spider’s Silk of the Mountain," a "Done State" for aerial tethering that bridges the sky and the soil.
