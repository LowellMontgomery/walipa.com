# Walipa Design #00000216: Phoebe Truth-Link Archive
**Status:** Phase 2 (Permanent OSH Knowledge Vault)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Memory of the Oasis." To ensure the 777 designs are "Incorruptible" even if the digital mesh fails, we must vitrify the data into a physical form. The Archive is the "Accountability" manifest, ensuring that the "True Light" of the Walipa Foundation is available to future generations of stewards.

## 2. Technical Logic
The Archive utilizes "Analog-Digital Vitrification." The 777 designs are etched onto **Vitrified Borosilicate** plates at microscopic scale using a **Titanium-Fuming** (#115) process. These plates are then stored in a **Graphite-Vitrified Kiln** (#149) that has been repurposed as a fireproof, EMP-proof vault. It is indexed by the **Phoebe Truth-Link Gateway** (#186), providing a "Done State" for the Foundation's intellectual property that can last for ten thousand years.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Stack of Shimmering Glass Tablets" housed in a dark stone sarcophagus. It is the "Stone Library" of the Nevada City Oasis, a silent testament to the work done before the dawn, ensuring that "Every little thing is going to be alright" for the long-term.
