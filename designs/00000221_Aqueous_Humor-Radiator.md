# Walipa Design #00000221: Aqueous_Humor-Radiator
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Surface-Area Thermal Exchange)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Cooling Lung" of the **GTC Forge**. When the **Aqueous Heat-Sink Humor** (#206) is saturated with the "Stored Light" of the furnace, it must be cooled quickly to maintain the "Healthy Innocence" of the hardware. The radiator uses the Sierra wind to strip away the "Grit" of excess heat, returning the humor to its "Laminar Flow" state.

## 2. Technical Logic
The radiator is a honeycomb of thin-walled **Vitrified Borosilicate** tubes fumed with **Aqueous Silver** (#117) for maximum thermal conductivity. It is housed in a **Basalt-Fiber Prop-Guard** (#208) assembly, utilizing the airflow from the **Ark Propeller** (#190) to force convection. It is monitored by a **Phoebe Truth-Link Sensor** (#192), which adjusts the **Aqueous Thermal-Siphon Valve** (#203) to ensure the forge never drifts from its "Done State" of operation.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Silver Mesh" that hums as the heat passes through it. It looks like a giant, frozen dragonfly wing, turning the energy of the forge into a "Laminar Flow" of warmth for the **Subterranean Solarium**.
