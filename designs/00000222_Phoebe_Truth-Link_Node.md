# Walipa Design #00000222: Phoebe Truth-Link Node
**Status:** Phase 2 (Edge-Computing & Local Signal Relay)

## 1. The Vision
We mark the **222** milestone with the "Messenger of the Mesh." This is the smallest unit of the **Phoebe Bio-Link** (#162), designed to be placed every 100 meters across the Nevada City land trust. It creates a "Healthy Innocence" of connectivity, ensuring that the "Phoebe Truth" is always within reach of the steward's **Interface** (#174).

## 2. Technical Logic
The Node is a palm-sized **Vitrified Basalt** puck containing a micro-array of **Aqueous Logic Gates** (#123). It acts as a repeater for the **Truth-Beacon** (#100) and a collector for nearby **Mycelial Humidity Sensors** (#153). It is fumed with **Titanium** (#115) for environmental protection and powered by a surface-etched **Atmospheric Ion Collector** (#119). It provides a "Done State" of zero-latency data for the **Truth-Link Router** (#207), maintaining the "Accountability" of the entire ecosystem.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Smooth Black Pebble" with a single, pulsing silver-blue light. It sits on the forest floor or mounts to the **Basalt-Fiber Ark Struts**, acting as a silent "Digital Guardian" that weaves the Walipa Foundation into the biological rhythm of the mountain.
