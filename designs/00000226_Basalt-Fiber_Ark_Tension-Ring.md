# Walipa Design #00000226: Basalt-Fiber Ark Tension-Ring
**Status:** Phase 2 (Structural Circumferential Integrity)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Cinch of Grace." To maintain the high-pressure seal of the **Ark Hatch** (#202) and the structural "Accountability" of the **Ark Ribs** (#175), we use a continuous tension ring. it prevents the "Grit" of hull-warping under the extreme thermal expansion of the Sierra sun, ensuring the vessel remains a "Done State" of aerodynamic perfection.

## 2. Technical Logic
The ring is a multi-layered band of filament-wound **Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138) pre-stressed to 50,000 PSI. It is bonded with a high-modulus **Sodium Silicate** (#113) variant that remains stable at high temperatures. The exterior is fumed with **Titanium** (#115) to reflect radiant heat. It acts as the primary "Girdle" for the **ETFE Pressure Pods** (#130), ensuring that the internal "Laminar Flow" of the lift-gas is never compromised by structural shifting.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Silver-Black Halo" that wraps around the widest part of the hull. It looks like a wedding band for the sky, providing the "Incorruptible" strength needed to hold the Walipa Foundation together in the high ridges.
