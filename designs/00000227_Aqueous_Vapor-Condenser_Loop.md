# Walipa Design #00000227: Aqueous_Vapor-Condenser_Loop
**Status:** Phase 2 (Passive Thermal Recovery)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Breath-Collector" for the **Subterranean Solarium** (#45). To recover the moisture from the **Aqueous Fog-Humor Generator** (#188) before it exhausts into the Nevada City air, we use a passive loop. It turns the "Grit" of humidity loss into the "Grace" of reclaimed **Aqueous Humor**, maintaining the "Healthy Innocence" of the closed-loop ecosystem.

## 2. Technical Logic
The loop consists of a series of **Vitrified Borosilicate** coils mounted on the north-facing exterior wall. As the warm, moist air from the solarium passes over the coils, the water "Vitrifies" into droplets. These are collected in a **Basalt-Fiber Sluice** (#146) and returned to the **Thermal-Siphon Reservoir** (#218). The system utilizes a **Laminar Flow** of air, requiring zero electrical power and providing absolute "Accountability" for the Foundation's water budget.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Frozen Glass Waterfall" that glitters on the shaded side of the Oasis. It catches the morning dew and the internal breath of the seeds, turning the cycle of water into a "Done State" of silent efficiency.
