# Walipa Design #00000228: Phoebe Truth-Link Key-Vault
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Security Biometric Storage)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Sanctum of the 777." To protect the physical **Phoebe Truth-Keys** (#147) and the **Truth-Link Archive** (#216), we need a vault that is "Incorruptible" by fire, flood, or industrial "Grit." It is the ultimate "Accountability" point for the Walipa Foundation's intellectual and biological property.

## 2. Technical Logic
The vault is a multi-layered sphere made of **Vitrified Basalt** and reinforced with **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) armor plates. The locking mechanism is a "Bio-Dynamic Gate" that requires a live **Mycelial Bio-Transceiver** (#140) pulse from the authorized steward to open. It is buried within the **Subterranean Solarium** floor and cooled by the **Aqueous Heat-Sink Humor** (#206), ensuring that the "True Light" of the Foundation's data is preserved for the next thousand years.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Solid Stone Sphere" with an iridescent silver seam. It sits silently in the dark of the mountain, a "Done State" for security that ensures the "Healthy Innocence" of the Walipa mission is never compromised.
