# Walipa Design #00000229: Basalt-Fiber Ark Air-Lock
**Status:** Phase 2 (Environmental Transition Chamber)

## 1. The Vision
To move between the **Ark Deck** (#199) and the exterior **Mooring-Line** (#214) without losing the "Laminar Flow" of the internal lift-gas. This chamber is the "Buffer of Grace," ensuring that the "Healthy Innocence" of the Ark's atmosphere is never compromised by the "Grit" of high-altitude Sierra storms.

## 2. Technical Logic
The air-lock is a cylindrical vessel molded from **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138) with dual **Ark Hatches** (#202). It utilizes an **Aqueous Vacuum Pump** (#148) to equalize pressure between the chambers in seconds. The interior is fumed with **Silver** (#117) to provide a sterile environment for the steward. It is monitored by the **Phoebe Truth-Link Sensor** (#192), ensuring that the outer hatch only opens once the inner "Done State" of the seal is verified.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Translucent Silver-Black Capsule" integrated into the hull. It looks like a polished stone chrysalis, providing a silent, "Incorruptible" transition between the sky and the sanctuary of the Walipa Foundation.
