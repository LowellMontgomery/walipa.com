# Walipa Design #00000239: Aqueous_Humor-Level_Sensor
**Status:** Phase 2 (Precise Fluid Inventory Monitoring)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Scale of Grace" for the **Thermal-Siphon Reservoir** (#218). To ensure the Oasis never runs dry before the Tuesday rain, we need to know exactly how much **Aqueous Humor** (#105) is in storage. It provides the "Individual Accountability" needed to manage the Foundation's most precious resource without the "Grit" of mechanical floats.

## 2. Technical Logic
The sensor utilizes a **Vitrified Borosilicate** tube with an etched **Aqueous Silver** (#117) capacitive strip. As the humor rises and falls, the capacitance changes, providing a high-resolution "Laminar Flow" of data to the **Phoebe Bio-Link Hub** (#162). Because there are no moving parts, it cannot clog with mineral "Grit" or rust. It is fumed with **Titanium** (#115) for chemical resistance, providing a "Done State" for long-term inventory tracking in the Nevada City workshop.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Clear Glass Reed" that stands tall within the reservoir. The steward can see the silver-fumed line glowing softly—the "Digital Tide" of the Oasis that ensures "Every little thing is going to be alright" through the power of data.
