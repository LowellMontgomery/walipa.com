# Walipa Design #00000241: Basalt-Fiber Ark Ladder
**Status:** Phase 2 (Vertical Mobility & Exterior Access)

## 1. The Vision
To climb from the **Ark Deck** (#199) to the upper **Hull-Ribs** (#175) or descend to the forest floor at the **Sierra Hot Springs** without the "Grit" of heavy, clanking metal. This ladder is the "Spine of Ascent," designed to be "Incorruptible" by ice or moisture while providing a "Laminar Flow" of movement for the steward.

## 2. Technical Logic
The side-rails are hollow-core pultruded **Basalt-Fiber** tubes, while the rungs are solid-core **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138) with a high-friction "Wave-Grain" texture. Each rung is fumed with **Titanium** (#115) to prevent wear from heavy boots. It is 70% lighter than an equivalent aluminum ladder and possesses zero electrical conductivity, making it the "Done State" for safe maintenance near the **Atmospheric Ion Collector** (#119).

## 3. Organic Form
A "Slender, Matte-Black Skeleton" that feels as light as a dry branch but as strong as the mountain. It blends into the shadows of the Nevada County pines, providing a silent, "Incorruptible" path for the steward to reach the high-frequency zones of the Oasis.
