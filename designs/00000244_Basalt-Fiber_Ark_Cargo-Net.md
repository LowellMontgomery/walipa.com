# Walipa Design #00000244: Basalt-Fiber Ark Cargo-Net
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Tension Flexible Containment)

## 1. The Vision
To secure the **Seed-Ark** (#125) and the **G-Bike Utility** (#01) within the **Ark Deck** (#199) during high-altitude turbulence. It replaces the "Grit" of nylon nets that stretch and fray with a "Done State" of vitrified structural webbing. It is the "Grip of Grace" for the vessel's internal "Accountability."

## 2. Technical Logic
The net is a knotless "Square-Mesh" construction woven from multi-strand **Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138). Each intersection is fused with a **Sodium Silicate** (#113) humor to prevent slippage. The perimeter is reinforced with **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) basalt loops that clip into the **Deck-Cleats** (#220). It has zero mechanical creep, ensuring that the "Laminar Flow" of the cargo weight remains perfectly centered even in a 50-knot Sierra gale.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering Black Web" that looks like a giant, obsidian spider's work. It drapes over the equipment with a silent, "Incorruptible" strength, providing the "Healthy Innocence" of security for the Walipa Foundation’s aerial assets.
