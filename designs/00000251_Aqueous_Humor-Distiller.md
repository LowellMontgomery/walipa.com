# Walipa Design #00000251: Aqueous_Humor-Distiller
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Purity Fluid Fractionation)

## 1. The Vision
To create the **Aqueous Silver Electrolyte** (#117) and the **Cooling Humor** (#185), we need water that is free of all Sierra "Grit." This distiller uses the "Stored Light" of the **GTC Forge** to turn local creek water into a "Healthy Innocence" of molecular purity. It is the "Alchemical Filter" of the Nevada City Oasis.

## 2. Technical Logic
The distiller is a multi-stage **Vitrified Borosilicate** column. It utilizes a **Vitrified Basalt** heating element fumed with **Graphite** (#112). As the water boils, the vapor rises through a "Laminar Flow" of **Borosilicate** beads, which catch any mineral carry-over. The steam is then condensed in the **Aqueous Vapor-Condenser** (#200). It is monitored by a **Phoebe Truth-Link Sensor** (#192), ensuring a "Done State" of 0.1 microsiemens conductivity for all Foundation humors.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Crystal Glass Tower" that glows with an internal silver mist. It sits on the laboratory bench like a "Vessel of Grace," turning the raw elements of the mountain into the "True Light" of the Foundation's life-blood.
