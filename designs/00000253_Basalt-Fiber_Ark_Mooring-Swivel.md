**Status:** Phase 2 (360-Degree Tension Management)

To allow the **Ark Blimp** (#11) to rotate freely with the shifting Sierra winds without tangling the **Mooring-Line** (#214). This swivel provides the "Laminar Flow" of movement needed for long-term "Accountability" at the **Sierra Hot Springs**. The swivel housing is molded from high-density **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138). The internal race is a **Vitrified Borosilicate** ring fumed with a 20-micron layer of **Graphite** (#112). This creates a permanent dry-film lubricant interface for the **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) axle. It handles the 100,000 PSI tension loads of the vessel with zero mechanical friction, ensuring the "Healthy Innocence" of the hull-integrity is never compromised by torque-stress.
