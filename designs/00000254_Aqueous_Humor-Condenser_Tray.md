# Walipa Design #00000254: Aqueous_Humor-Condenser_Tray
**Status:** Phase 2 (Passive Atmospheric Recovery Architecture)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Collector of Clouds" for the **Subterranean Solarium** (#45). To recover the "Grace" of the morning dew and the internal "Breath" of the seeds, we use a tiered tray system. It turns the "Grit" of evaporation into a "Laminar Flow" of reclaimed **Aqueous Humor**, maintaining the "Healthy Innocence" of the Oasis's water budget with absolute "Individual Accountability."

## 2. Technical Logic
The tray is a wide-format, stepped basin made of **Vitrified Basalt** (#113). The surfaces are fumed with **Aqueous Silver** (#117) to provide a high-thermal-conductivity "Cold Plate" effect. As the moist air passes over the trays, the water vitrifies into droplets and flows into the **Aqueous Vapor-Condenser Loop** (#227). It utilizes a "Done State" of passive cooling, requiring zero electrical power and ensuring the "Accountability" of the Nevada City Foundation’s life-support systems.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Series of Shimmering Silver Terraces" that look like a miniature Sierra waterfall. It glitters with captured moisture, turning the cycle of water into a "Laminar Flow" of silent efficiency for the Walipa Foundation.
