# Walipa Design #00000260: Aqueous_Humor-Siphon_Drain
**Status:** Phase 2 (Passive Fluid-Reclamation Outlet)

## 1. The Vision
This is the "Exit-Gate" for the **Aqueous Humor-Siphon** (#236). To reclaim the "Grace" of the condensed water without letting the "Grit" of external air back into the system, we need a one-way fluid-lock. It ensures the **Subterranean Solarium** (#45) stays in a state of "Healthy Innocence" while maintaining the water-budget before the Tuesday rain.

## 2. Technical Logic
The drain is a "Siphon-Trap" geometry made of **Vitrified Borosilicate**. It utilizes the weight of the **Aqueous Humor** itself to maintain a liquid seal against the internal vacuum. A **Titanium-Fumed** (#115) check-ball prevents back-flow during pressure fluctuations. It is a passive, "Done State" mechanism that directs the reclaimed humor back to the **Thermal-Siphon Reservoir** (#218) with 100% efficiency and zero maintenance.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Crystal Glass Loop" that pulses with a rhythmic drip. The steward can see the "Laminar Flow" of the water returning to the earth, acting as the "Mechanical Kidney" of the Foundation’s life-support systems.
