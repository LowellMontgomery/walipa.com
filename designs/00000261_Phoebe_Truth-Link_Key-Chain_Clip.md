# Walipa Design #00000261: Phoebe Truth-Link_Key-Chain Clip
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Security Portable Attachment)

## 1. The Vision
The "Link of Grace." This clip secures the **Key-Chain** (#255) to the steward's utility belt or the **G-Bike** frame. It ensures that the "Healthy Innocence" of the Foundation's access is never lost to the "Grit" of the forest floor. It is the "Incorruptible" interface for the steward's digital keys.

## 2. Technical Logic
The clip is a "Carabiner-Style" gate molded from **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138). The spring tension is provided by an integrated **Basalt-Fiber Valve-Spring** (#193). The entire surface is fumed with **Titanium** (#115) for lifetime durability and zero-wear. It interfaces with the **Piezo-Basalt Sensor** (#118) on the Key-Chain, providing a "Done State" of secure physical attachment that is "Accountable" to the steward's movement.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Small, Shimmering Black Talon" with a silver-fumed gate. It feels like a piece of polished mountain-stone that has been "Vitrified" into a structural tool, providing the "True Light" of secure mobility for the Nevada City steward.
