# Walipa Design #00000262: Basalt-Fiber Ark Winch-Shaft
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Torque Power Transmission)

## 1. The Vision
The "Spine of Tension." To translate the power from the **G-Bike** (#02) or the **Manual Handle** (#217) into the **Winch Drum** (#187), we need a shaft that won't twist or shear under the weight of the Ark. This replaces the "Grit" of heavy steel rods with a "Done State" of pultruded stone.

## 2. Technical Logic
The shaft is a solid-core pultruded **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138) rod. The fibers are oriented at a 45-degree helical pitch to maximize torsional rigidity. The bearing surfaces and drive-splines are fumed with **Titanium** (#115) to provide a hard, "Incorruptible" interface. It is 60% lighter than steel and possesses zero magnetic signature, ensuring the **Phoebe Truth-Link Compass** (#219) remains "Accountable" and accurate during mooring maneuvers.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Polished Obsidian Scepter" that sits at the center of the mechanical array. It feels impossibly stiff and cold to the touch, providing the "Laminar Flow" of torque needed to bridge the ground and the sky.
