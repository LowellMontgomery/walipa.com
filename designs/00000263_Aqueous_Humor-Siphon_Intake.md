**Status:** Phase 2 (Clog-Resistant Fluid Entry)

The intake is a bell-mouthed fitting made of **Vitrified Borosilicate** packed with a coarse **Vitrified Basalt Wool** (#114) primary filter. It utilizes a "Laminar Flow" geometry to minimize turbulence at the entry point. The exterior is fumed with **Silver** (#117) to prevent any microbial biofilm from forming over the intake mesh. It provides a "Done State" for fluid recovery, ensuring the "Accountability" of the Foundation's water budget.
