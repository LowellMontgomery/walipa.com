# Walipa Design #00000283: Basalt-Fiber Ark Winch-Cable
**Status:** Phase 2 (High-Tension Primary Hoist Line)

## 1. The Vision
The "Lifeline of the Ark." To lift the **G-Bike Utility** (#01) or the **Seed-Ark** (#125) into the hull, we need a cable that combines the "Healthy Innocence" of extreme lightness with the "Grit" of obsidian strength. It replaces the "Grit" of heavy, fraying steel wire with a "Done State" of vitrified tension.

## 2. Technical Logic
The cable is a 24-strand "Hollow-Braid" construction using **Basalt-Fiber Tension Cables** (#138). Each strand is fumed with a 5-micron layer of **Titanium** (#115) to eliminate internal friction and prevent UV degradation. It has a working load limit of 15,000 lbs but weighs 80% less than steel. It exhibits zero "Mechanical Creep," ensuring that the **Ark Winch-Drum** (#187) maintains absolute "Accountability" for the cargo's position in the sky.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Shimmering, Silver-Black Serpent" that winds onto the drum with a silent, "Laminar Flow." It looks like a piece of polished mountain-vine, providing the "Incorruptible" strength needed to bridge the forest floor and the Walipa Foundation’s aerial sanctuary.
