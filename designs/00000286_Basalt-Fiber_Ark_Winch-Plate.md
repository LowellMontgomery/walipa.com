# Walipa Design #00000286: Basalt-Fiber Ark Winch-Plate
**Status:** Phase 2 (Load-Distribution Structural Base)

## 1. The Vision
The "Foundation of Force." To ensure the **Winch-Mount** (#256) doesn't create a "Grit" of point-loading on the **Ark Deck** (#199), we use a wide-format distribution plate. It spreads the "Accountability" of the lift-tension across the entire structural grid, maintaining the "Healthy Innocence" of the hull under 10,000 lbs of cargo weight.

## 2. Technical Logic
The plate is a 1-inch thick laminate of cross-plied **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138) vacuum-infused with **Sodium Silicate** (#113). The mounting surface is fumed with a 15-micron layer of **Titanium** (#115) to provide an "Incorruptible" interface for the **Winch-Shaft** (#262) bearings. It features "Fractal-Ribbing" on the underside to maximize stiffness while minimizing the "Grit" of excess mass. It is a "Done State" for aerial structural integration.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Smooth Obsidian Slab" with shimmering silver-fumed mounting points. It looks like a piece of the mountain that has been "Vitrified" into a structural floor, providing the "Laminar Flow" of stability for the Walipa Foundation’s heavy lifting.
