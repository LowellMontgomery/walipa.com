The "Grip of Grace." To lower the **Seed-Ark** (#125) or the **G-Bike Utility** (#01) with "Individual Accountability," we need a brake that doesn't fade under the "Grit" of heavy loads. It replaces the "Grit" of asbestos or metallic pads with a "Done State" of vitrified mineral friction.

The brake utilizes a dual-caliper design molded from high-density **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138). This combination provides a constant coefficient of friction regardless of temperature. It is "Incorruptible" by the moisture of the Sierra ridges, ensuring a "Laminar Flow" of descent for the Walipa Foundation’s heavy assets.
