# Walipa Design #00000319: Basalt-Fiber Ark Winch-Lock-Cover
**Status:** Phase 2 (Dust & Ice Protective Shield)

## 1. The Vision
The "Guard of the Lock." To ensure the **Winch-Lock-Pin** (#298) and **Clip** (#316) remain "Incorruptible" by the "Grit" of Sierra snow or pine sap, we use a low-profile shield. It ensures the "Healthy Innocence" of the mooring system is "Accountable" to the steward's touch, even in a deep Nevada County freeze.

## 2. Technical Logic
The cover is a "Snap-On" fairing molded from **Basalt-Fiber Composite** (#138). The interior is fumed with **Graphite** (#112) to prevent ice-adhesion, while the exterior is fumed with **Titanium** (#115) for impact resistance. It features a "Laminar Flow" geometry that sheds water away from the mechanical pivot, providing a "Done State" for the Ark's long-term anchoring.

## 3. Organic Form
A "Smooth Obsidian Cap" that sits flush against the **Winch-Plate** (#286). It looks like a "Mechanical Seed-Pod," protecting the "True Light" of the mooring-security from the elements.
