# Walipa Design #03: Molecular Sieve (Aqueous Purification)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Gravity Column)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)
**Version:** 1.0.6

## 1. Purpose
To strip "Grit" (endocrine disruptors, PFAS, and pharmaceutical residues) from the planetary water supply using physical and ionic adsorption.

## 2. Components
- **The Column:** Precision borosilicate glass or vitrified ceramic.
- **The Media:**
    - **Sieve:** 3Å/4Å Zeolite crystals.
    - **Adsorbent:** Activated charcoal (Walipa wood-gasification byproduct).
    - **Polishing:** Ionic silver-impregnated ceramic beads.

## 3. Implementation
Designed as a gravity-fed "Pre-Processor" for all community drinking and irrigation systems.
