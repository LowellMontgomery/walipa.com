# Walipa Design #04: Graphite Vaporizer (G-Vape)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Transition Tool)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)
**Version:** 1.1.2

## 1. Overview
A convection-based delivery system for botanical resins and fungal extracts, facilitating the transition from combustion (smoke) to pure sublimation.

## 2. Components
- **Heat Element:** Precision-machined graphite thermal core.
- **Air Path:** 100% borosilicate glass (no heavy metals or plastics in the vapor path).
- **Housing:** Sodium-silicate treated hemp composite.

## 3. Logic
By maintaining a stable 180°C - 210°C temperature via the graphite core, plant medicine is delivered without the "Grit" of tar or carbon monoxide.
