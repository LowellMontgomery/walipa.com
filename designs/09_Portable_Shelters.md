# Walipa Design #09: Portable Shelters (The Hub)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Deployable Architecture)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)
**Version:** 1.0.9

## 1. Overview
Lightweight, rapid-deployment shelter systems that replace toxic plastics with ETFE and sodium-silicate treated hemp.

## 2. Models
- **The Greenhouse:** Full ETFE skin for high-transparency food production.
- **The Living Pod:** Mylar-lined hemp skin for extreme thermal reflection.
- **The Skeleton:** Folding basalt-fiber or high-tensile bamboo frame.
