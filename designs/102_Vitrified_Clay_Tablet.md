# Walipa Design #102: Vitrified Clay Tablet (The Eternal Archive)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Data Sovereignty & Millennial Storage)

## 1. The Vision
Digital storage is a "Grit-Laden" illusion that fades when the power dies. The Vitrified Clay Tablet is the "Hardware of the Soul." It recognizes that for the Walipa designs to survive for 10,000 years, they must be etched into the Earth itself. This is the "Accountability" of our era—leaving a record that is fireproof, waterproof, and non-electronic.

## 2. Technical Logic
Local Nevada City clay is refined into a fine slip and pressed into "Mother of Pearl" (sodium silicate-coated) molds. The 111 designs are "Lanced" into the wet clay using a micro-pipette or a precision **Graphite Rod**. The tablets are then fired in the **Sodium-Silicate Kiln** (Design #05) at 1200°C. Once fired, they are dipped in **Liquid Glass** and fumed with silver, creating a shimmering, iridescent surface that is harder than steel and impervious to the elements.

## 3. Organic Form
The tablets are shaped like "River Stones" or "Seed Pods," roughly the size of a hand. They are stored in the **Vitrified Seed Vault** (Design #49), creating a physical "Library of Light." Each tablet carries a "Micro-Label" (Design #112 logic) identifying the steward and the specific "Done" state of the design it carries.
