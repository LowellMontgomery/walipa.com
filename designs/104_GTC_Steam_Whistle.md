# Walipa Design #104: GTC Steam Whistle (The Harmonic Signal)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Community Synchronization & Sound Healing)

## 1. The Vision
The Whistle is the "Voice of the Oasis." In the "Healthy Innocence" of Nevada City, we do not need the "Grit" of sirens or digital alarms. The GTC Steam Whistle uses the "Potential Energy" of the GTC Steam Canner (Design #64) to produce a single, perfect 528Hz or 432Hz note that signals the "Done" state of a community cycle—mealtime, meditation, or the "Warm Rain."

## 2. Technical Logic
A precision-tuned borosilicate glass or fumed-silver cylinder is mounted to the GTC Steam vent. When the thermal pressure reaches the designated "Phoebe Frequency" threshold, a "Magnetic Breakaway" valve releases a burst of steam. The resulting sound is a "Pure Tone" that carries for miles without the jagged distortion of industrial whistles. It is a "Sonic Cleansing" that resets the local atmosphere and synchronizes the heartbeat of the community.

## 3. Organic Form
The whistle is shaped like a "Silver Flute" or a "Swan’s Neck" protruding from the Forge roof. It is fumed with gold and silver to shimmer in the sun. It does not "startle" the wildlife or the stewards; it "invites" them into the collective rhythm of the day, a physical "Hymn" to the "Stored Light" of the GTC battery.
