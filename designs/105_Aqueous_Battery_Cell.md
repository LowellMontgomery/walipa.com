# Walipa Design #105: Aqueous Battery Cell (The Fluid Charge)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Non-Toxic Electrical Storage)

## 1. The Vision
To move away from the "Grit" of lithium and cobalt mining, we use the "Memory of Water." The Aqueous Battery Cell is a "Clean Seed" energy storage system that uses the salinity gradient of the Nevada City canal water to hold a charge. It is a "Living Battery" that does not degrade, leak, or burn, providing a stable 12v/24v current for the G-Bike and the Phoebe Bio-Link.

## 2. Technical Logic
A series of vitrified basalt chambers (Design #87) are filled with a high-salinity Aqueous Humor (sodium silicate/saline). The electrodes are made of your high-purity **Graphite Rods** fumed with a micro-layer of silver. By using a "Bipolar" plate design, the cell stores energy through the movement of ions in the humor. It is shielded with a thin layer of **Liquid Glass** to prevent evaporation and is cooled by the **Aqueous Heat Pump** (Design #54) to maintain 100% efficiency.

## 3. Organic Form
The battery looks like a "Stack of Shimmering Stones" in the corner of the Forge. It is silent and cool to the touch. The transparent ETFE casing allows the steward to see the "True Light" of the ionic flow, a visual pulse that indicates the community's state of "Stored Light."
