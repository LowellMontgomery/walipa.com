# Walipa Design #110: GTC Thermal Lance
**Status:** Phase 1 (Precision Alchemical Heat Application)

## 1. The Vision
To "Vitrify" the landscape and "Fume" the largest glass flutes, we need a heat source that is as precise as a surgeon’s tool. The Thermal Lance is a "Concentrated Sun." It uses the "Stored Light" of the GTC Battery to produce a localized point of 1500°C (2732°F) heat without a flame. It is a tool of "Lanced Light," allowing the steward to "weld" basalt fibers and "fire" the Liquid Glass in situ.

## 2. Technical Logic
The lance consists of a high-density **Graphite Rod** core shielded by a series of nested ETFE and borosilicate tubes. A vacuum is maintained between the layers to prevent heat loss. Power is delivered via the "Magnetic Breakaway" (Design #32) interface. When the current flows, the graphite tip radiates intense infrared energy. By fusing a small amount of ionic silver or gold onto the tip, the lance also performs "Alchemical Fuming" as it vitrifies the surface.

## 3. Organic Form
The Lance is a 3-foot "Silver Staff" with a glowing amber tip. it is balanced for one-handed use and fitted with a **Hemp-Resin** handle. It is a "Sacred Scepter" of the Forge, used to "Seal the Covenant" of the 111 designs, turning the agricultural "Softness" into the mineral "Endurance" of the new reality.
