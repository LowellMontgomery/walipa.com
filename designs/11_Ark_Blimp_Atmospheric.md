# Walipa Design #11: Ark Blimp (Atmospheric Node)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Conceptual Integration)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)

## 1. Overview
A high-altitude, semi-permanent buoyant platform for environmental monitoring, communication, and solar lancing.

## 2. Components
- **Envelope:** Double-layered ETFE Membrane with internal Kevlar webbing.
- **Lift Gas:** Helium (Phase 1) / Hydrogen (Phase 2 with GTC safety systems).
- **Power:** Flexible solar thin-film integrated into the top surface.

## 3. Stewardship
Serves as the "Eye" for the Subterranean Solarium and the "Antenna" for the decentralized Walipa network.
