# Walipa Design #13: Hemp-Silicate Masonry (Bio-Stone)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Material Mix)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)

## 1. Overview
A carbon-negative building block created by mixing hemp hurd, lime, and sodium silicate.

## 2. Logic
Unlike standard hempcrete, the addition of sodium silicate (liquid glass) accelerates the "petrification," creating a fireproof, pest-resistant stone within hours.
