# Walipa Design #15: GTC Thermal Battery (Storage)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Storage Module)
**License:** Open Source Holistic (Stewardship Covenant)

## 1. Overview
A static, large-scale Graphite Thermal Capacitor for storing solar or wind energy as heat.

## 2. Performance
Can maintain 1200°C temperatures with less than 2% loss per 24 hours when vacuum-insulated in a Subterranean Solarium.
