# Walipa Design #44: Hemp Cellulose Film
**Status:** Phase 1 (Membrane)
**Logic:** A biodegradable alternative to plastic eaves-screens. Made from fermented hemp and sealed with a thin coat of liquid glass.
**Form:** A shimmering, semi-transparent membrane that allows wasps to pass through designated gaps while excluding mosquitoes.
