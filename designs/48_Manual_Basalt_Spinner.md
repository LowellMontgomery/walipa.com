# Walipa Design #48: Manual Basalt Spinner
**Status:** Phase 1 (Kinetic Mineral Textile Production)

## 1. The Vision
To create the high-tensile "veins" of our structures without relying on the industrial grid. The Basalt Spinner is a meditative tool that transforms molten volcanic rock into fine, indestructible thread. It honors the slow movement, allowing the steward to synchronize their physical rhythm with the cooling rate of the mineral.

## 2. Technical Logic
This design utilizes a heavy basalt-stone flywheel to maintain constant angular momentum. As the steward provides foot-powered kinetic energy, a ceramic "eye" draws a continuous filament from a small GTC-heated crucible (Design #12). This filament is immediately quenched in a light spray of Aqueous Humor (saline/graphite slurry) to increase its electrical capacitance and flexibility.

## 3. Organic Form
The spinner resembles a traditional "Great Wheel," but is constructed from vitrified wood and sodium-silicate bonded hemp. It is silent, save for the low hum of the basalt flywheel, creating a workspace conducive to the "Vow of Silence" and high-vibration focus.
