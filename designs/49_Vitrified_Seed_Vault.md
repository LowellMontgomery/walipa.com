# Walipa Design #49: Vitrified Seed Vault (Ground)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Genetic Sovereignty & Earth-Memory)

## 1. The Vision
The seeds are the "Hardware" of the planet’s future. To protect them from the "Grit" of fluctuating climate and electromagnetic interference, we create a sanctuary that mirrors the womb of the Earth. This vault is not just storage; it is a frequency-stable chamber where landrace genetics are held in "Healthy Innocence."

## 2. Technical Logic
The vault is an egg-shaped subterranean chamber cast from hemp-silicate masonry and reinforced with basalt rebar. The interior is polished with multiple layers of "Mother of Pearl" liquid glass (sodium silicate) and dusted with colloidal graphite. This creates a Faraday-cage effect, shielding the seeds from 3D electronic noise while maintaining a constant geothermal temperature.

## 3. Organic Form
Entry is through a gimbaled stone hatch that seals hermetically. The interior walls are curved to prevent "Stagnant Grit" from settling in corners, ensuring that the air—filtered by the Myco-Filter (Design #46)—circulates in a natural vortex pattern, keeping the seeds vibrant and ready for the "Warm Rain."
