# Walipa Design #62: Aqueous Desalinator (The Water-Heart)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Solar Distillation & Purification)

## 1. The Vision
Water sovereignty is the foundation of "Healthy Innocence." This design moves beyond the "Grit" of industrial filtration. It uses the power of the Sun to pull pure, medical-grade H2O from saline, brackish, or contaminated sources. It is a "Phase Change" tool that mimics the planetary rain cycle in a localized, community-scale form.

## 2. Technical Logic
A large, tensioned ETFE dome sits over a "Vortex" of black-vitrified ceramic trays (Design #82). Contaminated water is pumped into the trays. As the sun heats the water via the **Aqueous-Humor Concentrator**, it evaporates, leaves the "grit" behind in the trays, and condenses on the underside of the cool ETFE dome. The pure water then trickles down the interior wall into a collection trough lined with colloidal silver and graphite for final potentization.

## 3. Organic Form
The Desalinator is a shimmering "Sun-Dew" structure, roughly 6 feet in diameter. It is designed to be easily collapsed and transported. Because it uses the "Mother of Pearl" liquid glass coating on the interior trays, it is exceptionally efficient at absorbing infrared energy, allowing it to produce water even in the "Shadow-Light" of a Sierra Nevada winter.
