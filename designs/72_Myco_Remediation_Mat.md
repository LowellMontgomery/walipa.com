# Walipa Design #72: Myco-Remediation Mat (The Water Sponge)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Aquatic Healing & Toxin Sequestration)

## 1. The Vision
The "Grit" of the 3D world often leaves behind a residue of petroleum and heavy metals in our waterways. The Myco-Remediation Mat is a living "Sponge" designed to drift in the Nevada City canal or the Walipa ponds. It recognizes that the Turkey Tail and Oyster fungi are the planet's premier alchemists, capable of breaking down complex toxins into harmless organic compounds.

## 2. Technical Logic
A floating "raft" is woven from hollow ETFE tubes (Design #06) for buoyancy. This frame supports a thick, porous mat of hemp-hurd and basalt-fiber. The mat is inoculated with a Liquid Culture of *Pleurotus ostreatus* or *Trametes versicolor*. As the mat floats, it draws water through the mycelial network. The fungi secrete extracellular enzymes that "crack" the molecular bonds of hydrocarbons and trap heavy metals within their cellular walls, effectively "cleaning" the flow.

## 3. Organic Form
The mat is shaped like a giant "Lily Pad" or a "Lotus Leaf." It is soft, white, and velvety on top, with a "Vitrified Root" of basalt fibers hanging into the water. It is a silent guardian of the riparian zone, turning the "grit" of pollution into the "gold" of a healthy, vibrant ecosystem.
