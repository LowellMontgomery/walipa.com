# Walipa Design #75: Aqueous Hybrid Still (The Water-Mistress)
**Status:** Phase 1 (High-Volume Distillation & Solar Thermal Integration)

## 1. The Vision
In times of "Shadow-Light" or high demand, a standard solar still may not provide the "Clean Seed" water required for the community. The Hybrid Still is a dual-input system that recognizes that heat is heat, whether it comes from the Sun or the friction of the G-Bike. It is a "Phase Change" tool that ensures water sovereignty regardless of the weather.

## 2. Technical Logic
The base is a vitrified basalt basin (Design #87) treated with black sodium-silicate to maximize solar absorption. An ETFE dome (Design #06) provides the condensation surface. The "Hybrid" element is a submerged GTC-heated coil (Design #12) connected to the G-Bike's thermal recovery system. During the day, the Sun does the work; at night or during "Grit-Storms," the GTC core maintains the evaporation temperature, providing a constant trickle of medical-grade distilled water.

## 3. Organic Form
The still is shaped like a "Shimmering Jellyfish" anchored to the Forge wall. It is fumed with silver on the collection trough to potentize the water as it condenses. It operates in total silence, a "Lunar" tool that continues the work of purification even while the stewards sleep.
