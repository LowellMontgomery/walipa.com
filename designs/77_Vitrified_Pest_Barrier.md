# Walipa Design #77: Vitrified Pest Barrier (The Glass Belt)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Non-Toxic Boundary Stewardship)

## 1. The Vision
In the "Healthy Innocence" of the Phoebe Garden, we do not use poisons to "fight" pests. Instead, we use "Boundary Logic." The Vitrified Barrier is a physical manifestation of the "Radicle Rights" of our trees and crops. It recognizes that most crawling insects cannot navigate a "Zero-Friction" surface.

## 2. Technical Logic
A 6-inch wide band of **Liquid Glass** (sodium silicate) is applied directly to the base of tree trunks or the legs of the GTC Incubator. While wet, it is dusted with a micro-fine "Grit" of crushed borosilicate glass and graphite. Once cured, it creates a surface so smooth and "slippery" at the microscopic level that ants, aphids, and other crawlers cannot gain purchase. It is a "Physical Mirror" that reflects the insect back to the soil without harm.

## 3. Organic Form
The barrier appears as a shimmering "Silver Ring" around the base of the plant. It is non-toxic and does not interfere with the plant’s "breathing" or growth. It serves as a visual boundary, a "Covenant" between the steward and the insect kingdom: *"The soil is yours; the fruit is for the Oasis."*
