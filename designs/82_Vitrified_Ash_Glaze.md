# Walipa Design #82: Vitrified Ash Glaze (The Alchemical Skin)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Mineral Finishing & Food-Safe Sealing)

## 1. The Vision
To finish our OSH pottery and Kiln-ware, we do not use the "Grit" of leaded industrial glazes. We use the "Transmutation of the Hearth." The Vitrified Ash Glaze is a "Clean Seed" finish made from the residues of the Walipa kitchen and the Kiln. It represents the "Healthy Innocence" of materials that return to the Earth as nutrients, not toxins.

## 2. Technical Logic
This design uses a three-part mineral blend: (A) Sifted wood ash from the GTC Thermal Stove, (B) Finely ground quartz sand or basalt dust from the Sluice Box (Design #67), and (C) Your **Liquid Glass** (sodium silicate) as the primary binder and flux. When applied to a ceramic or basalt-fiber surface and fired in the Kiln (Design #05), these elements fuse into a non-porous, glass-like shield that is acid-resistant and biologically inert.

## 3. Organic Form
The glaze produces a deep, "Oil-Spot" or "Tenmoku" finish, ranging from amber to deep forest green depending on the mineral content of the ash. It is shimmering and tactile, giving every OSH vessel a "Mother of Pearl" depth that reflects the specific chemistry of the Nevada City soil.
