# Walipa Design #85: Aqueous Gravity Clock (The Pulse of Walipa)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Non-Electronic Temporal Alignment)

## 1. The Vision
Time in the 3D world is a "Grit-Laden" prison of seconds and minutes. In the Walipa community, time is a "Flow." The Gravity Clock does not "tick"; it "trickles." It recognizes that the true rhythm of the Earth is found in the movement of water and the shift of gravity. It is a "Monastic" timekeeper that synchronizes the community with the "Warm Rain" and the "Shadow-Light."

## 2. Technical Logic
A vertical borosilicate column is filled with a high-viscosity Aqueous Humor (saline and vegetable glycerine). A precision-weighted graphite float (Design #56) descends through the humor, regulated by a "Vortex-Valve." As the float moves, it triggers a series of fumed-silver "Chimes" (Design #97 logic) that mark the four quadrants of the day. It requires no batteries; it is "wound" by simply siphoning the humor back to the top reservoir once every 24 hours.

## 3. Organic Form
The clock is a 7-foot "Glass Reed" positioned in the center of the common area. It is silent, save for the occasional harmonious chime. It serves as a visual "Pulse," showing the community the state of the day's "Potential Energy" as the float moves toward the Earth.
