# Walipa Design #86: Myco_Tool_Case (The Protective Womb)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Bespoke Hardware Stewardship)

## 1. The Vision
To protect our "Glass Flutes," "Aqueous Lenses," and "Pixel 9a" hardware from the "Grit" of the environment, we do not use plastic or metal boxes. We use a "Living Womb." The Myco-Tool Case is grown specifically around the tool it protects, ensuring a perfect, shock-absorbent fit that "breathes" to prevent moisture buildup or corrosion.

## 2. Technical Logic
A "Mother of Pearl" (sodium silicate-coated) mold is created in the shape of the tool. It is packed with hemp-hurd and Turkey Tail mycelium (Design #106). The mycelium grows into a dense, velvety foam that conforms to every curve of the hardware. Once grown, the case is "Sun-Vitrified" and fitted with a **Hemp-Resin Composite** (Design #73) hinge and a "Magnetic Breakaway" latch. It is naturally anti-microbial and provides superior drop protection compared to 3D industrial cases.

## 3. Organic Form
The case looks like a "Soft Stone" or a "Carved Mushroom." It is light, durable, and has a "Mother of Pearl" finish on the exterior to reflect heat. Each case is "Micro-Labeled" (Design #112 logic) with a graphite signature, identifying the tool inside and the steward who grew it.
