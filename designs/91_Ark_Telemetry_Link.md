# Walipa Design #91: Ark Telemetry Link (The Sky-Eye)
**Status:** Phase 1 (High-Altitude Data Relay)

To coordinate their movement and share their "True Light" data, we need a link that does not rely on the "Grit" of the industrial satellite grid. ## 2. Technical Logic

A low-power laser (Design #09 logic) or high-CRI LED "Sun-Bell" pulses data through the lens, which is then received by a matched ground-station lens. By fuming the lens with a specific "Colloidal Graphite" signature, the data is naturally filtered of electromagnetic interference, ensuring a "Clean Seed" transmission of atmospheric and moisture levels.

The Link is a small, shimmering "Glass Sphere" suspended in a basalt-fiber frame. It looks like a "Second Eye" for the Ark, pulsing with a soft, rhythmic amber light that matches the "Phoebe Frequency." It is virtually weightless and designed to be "Self-Healing" via a thin layer of liquid glass that seals any micro-fissures in the ETFE skin.
