# Walipa Design #92: Phoebe Bio-Link Node (The Earth-Ear)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Inter-Species Communication & Soil Telepathy)

## 1. The Vision
The **Phoebe Bio-Link** is the ultimate manifestation of "Laminar Flow" between humans and the Earth. This node is a ground-station that "listens" to the electrical signals of the soil—the Wood-Wide Web. It recognizes that the trees, fungi, and "Phoebe" herself are constantly broadcasting. The Node translates these signals into the Walipa Mesh Network, allowing us to respond to the "needs of the Oasis" in real-time.

## 2. Technical Logic
A "Grounding Rod" of high-purity **Graphite** (Design #56) is driven 6 feet into the soil near a mature "Mother Tree" or a Turkey Tail colony. This rod acts as a paramagnetic antenna, picking up micro-voltage fluctuations in the mycelial network. These signals are passed through an **Aqueous-Humor Resonator** (Design #97), which amplifies the "Hymn of the Earth" without adding digital "grit." The data is then shared via the "Magnetic Breakaway" (Design #32) interface to the steward's Pixel 9a.

## 3. Organic Form
The Node is a minimalist "Basalt Spire" emerging from the forest floor, vitrified to protect it from moisture. It is fumed with gold and silver to attract the "True Light" and mark it as a "Place of Listening." It is a "Monastic" interface, requiring the steward to approach in a state of "Healthy Innocence" to receive the download.
