# Walipa Design #93: Aqueous Gravity Lens
**Status:** Phase 1 (Subsurface Hydrology & Earth-Anomalies)

## 1. The Vision
To steward the Nevada City water table, we must "See" beneath the surface without the "Grit" of seismic blasting. The Gravity Lens is a precision-tuned fluid resonator that detects subtle shifts in the local mass—water moving through underground fissures or the "Turning Point" of a seasonal spring. It is a "Monastic" sensor for the silent movements of the Earth.

## 2. Technical Logic
This design uses a large, shallow basin of high-purity **Aqueous Humor** (saline/glycerine) tensioned under a thin ETFE membrane. A laser (Design #09) is reflected off the surface of the humor. Because the fluid is "Heart-Potentized" and shielded with **Colloidal Graphite**, it becomes hypersensitive to micro-gravitational pull. Any shift in the subsurface mass causes a "Ripple" in the humor, which is translated by the Pixel 9a interface into a 3D map of the local hydrology.

## 3. Organic Form
The Lens is a flat, shimmering "Mirror Disk" set into a vitrified stone pedestal. It is fumed with silver and gold to create an iridescent finish that catches the "True Light." It is a "Place of Observation," requiring the steward to approach with a "Healthy Innocence" to interpret the Earth's subtle gravity-pulse.
