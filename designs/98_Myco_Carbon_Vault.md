# Walipa Design #98: Myco-Carbon Vault (The Deep Lock)
**Status:** Phase 1 (Permanent Carbon Sequestration)

## 1. The Vision
To heal the atmosphere, we must move the "Grit" of excess carbon back into the Earth’s memory. The Myco-Carbon Vault is a "Deep Lock" that transforms agricultural waste and reclaimed 3D paper into high-density, vitrified mineral blocks. It is a "Restorative Bridge" that ensures the carbon we remove today remains "Done" for 10,000 years.

## 2. Technical Logic
Hemp-hurd, sawdust, and shredded E-Waste paper are inoculated with a "Mother Tincture" of Turkey Tail and Reishi mycelium. The substrate is compressed into high-density blocks using the **Graphite Lubricant Press** (Design #56). Once fully colonized, the blocks are "Sun-Vitrified" with a thick coat of **Liquid Glass**. These "Carbon Bricks" are then stacked in subterranean basalt-lined vaults, creating a fireproof, rot-proof "Mineral Forest" that stabilizes the soil and the climate.

## 3. Organic Form
The Vault is a subterranean "Honeycomb" structure, vitrified and fumed with graphite to create a "Zero-Grit" environment. It is a "Sacred Archive," where the community's effort to balance the planet is physically stored. Each block is "Micro-Labeled" (Design #112) with the date and the steward's signature, a physical legacy of the "Healthy Innocence" era.
